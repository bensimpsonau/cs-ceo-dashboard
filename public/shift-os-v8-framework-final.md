### 04 — Risk Management

Four types of risk: Market (price drops), Security (loss of assets), Liquidity (can't exit), Emotional (decisions you regret). The position sizing rule: only allocate what you can watch drop 50% without changing strategy. Different risk profiles for different allocation sizes — 10% of net worth vs 50%+ in crypto are completely different games. Staged entry. DCA vs lump sum.

> *"If your goal is quite big and if you're only going to put in a small amount that you're comfortable with, that's absolutely fine — but then it's also realising that your crypto, just being realistic, might only get you 30 or 40 percent towards your outcome."* — Ben Simpson

### 10 — Tax, Structure & Partners

**🇦🇺 Australia:** Every disposal = CGT event. 12+ month hold → 50% CGT discount. SMSF: 15% tax (0% pension phase). Family Trust: distribute to lower-tax beneficiaries. Company: 25% base rate entities / 30% others.

**🇳🇿 New Zealand:** No formal CGT — but IRD taxes crypto profits as ordinary income based on intent. If you bought crypto hoping it goes up, IRD generally treats profits as taxable. Documentation of intent is critical.

**🇺🇸 United States:** 12+ month hold → long-term capital gains rates. Self-directed IRA (traditional = tax-deferred, Roth = tax-free growth). US citizens taxed on worldwide income — cross-border specialist essential if living abroad.

**Universal:** Record keeping is everything — date, amount, value in local currency, purpose. Start with Koinly or CryptoTaxCalculator NOW, not at tax time. CS connects members with crypto-specialist advisors in each country.

**Four Pillars:**
- 📊 Research: Tax guides updated annually
- 📚 Education: Tax planning module, country-specific workshops
- 👥 Community: Tax partner referrals from other members
- 🎯 Coaching: Accountant introductions via CSM

**Deliverable:** Tax tool set up + advisor conversation booked

### 11 — Bitcoin Lending & Yield

Stablecoin yield spectrum — four tiers of risk:
- **Tier 1 (2-5% APY):** Tokenised treasury bills, Aave/Compound. Safest. Battle-tested smart contracts.
- **Tier 2 (5-9%):** Established centralised platforms, Curve Finance. Moderate. Counterparty risk.
- **Tier 3 (9-18%):** Maple Finance / syrupUSDC — where CS sits. Competitive yield, institutional borrowers, permissioned lending. Credit risk exists but managed.
- **Tier 4 (18-24%+):** High-yield CeFi, newer protocols. Highest risk — 2022 showed how quickly these freeze withdrawals.

The CS approach: earn competitive yield at a risk level we understand. 33.4% of our portfolio in syrupUSDC — earning while waiting for the 200-week MA. "Patience + yield. That's smart capital management."

**The key question:** Where does the yield come from? If you can't answer it clearly, you probably shouldn't be in it yet.

### 12 — Sell Strategy & Profit Taking

**The most important component. The capstone of everything.**

"How many of you have a written sell strategy right now?" Most don't — and it's the most expensive gap.

**Three trigger types:**
1. **Price-based:** Scale out at targets (10% at 2×, 15% at 3×, 20% at 5×, 25-30% at cycle peak signals, hold 25-35% forever)
2. **Condition-based:** Thesis changes, fundamentals shift, liquidity tightens
3. **Time-based:** Cycle milestones, personal goals reached

**Three questions for every position:**
1. What's my target?
2. What's my timeframe?
3. What would change my mind?

On-chain peak signals: NUPL >0.75, MVRV >3.5-4 — CS will flag these. Your job is to have the plan ready.

> *"I've been in crypto for nine years. I've met so many people that try to time the exact top, and it's like, who are you trying to prove this to? No one cares if you time the exact top... I've never met anyone in crypto that's taken significant profits and looked back and had deep regrets."* — Ben Simpson
