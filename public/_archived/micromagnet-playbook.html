Jeremy's Core Principle (Skill #16): "Marginal results at low price create desire for full results at high price." The lead magnet gives them a personalised score, strategy, or calculation. It's genuinely useful but raises more questions than it answers. "Your portfolio allocation score is 4/10... what do I do about that?" That question is answered by Platinum. The lead magnet satisfies curiosity for 24-72 hours. After that, the desire for the FULL solution returns. This is when the setter outreach or email sequence hits.
Critical Conflict Flag: Don't give away too much in the lead magnet. If the tool does everything Platinum does, there's no reason to upgrade. The tool should diagnose; Platinum should prescribe and implement. Each lead magnet must create a specific knowledge gap that only Platinum fills.

The 5 Micromagnets Ben-Approved Hooks
Each micromagnet has a keyword trigger, a hook, a ManyChat flow, and a specific ascension path to Platinum. The knowledge gap each creates is intentionally different, targeting different psychological states of the same ICP.

1. Personalised Digital Asset Strategy

STRATEGY
What It Is

GPT-powered tool that asks 5-7 questions about portfolio size, risk tolerance, time horizon, and goals, then generates a personalised digital asset strategy outline in 3 minutes. Shows what a framework looks like without giving the full implementation playbook.

Hook (Post Copy)

"I built a tool that creates your personalised digital asset strategy in 3 minutes. Comment STRATEGY and I'll send it to you."

Knowledge Gap Created

"I can see what a strategy looks like, but I don't know how to actually build and implement it for my specific situation."

Platinum Bridge

"Platinum members get this strategy built, implemented, and managed with ongoing support from our team."

Claims Separation

Magnet: "See what a strategy looks like." Platinum: "We'll build your entire digital asset infrastructure."

ManyChat Flow (Tim Build Spec)
  • Trigger: Comment "STRATEGY" on any post OR DM keyword
  • DM 1: "Hey [name]! Here's the strategy tool. Before I send it, quick question: roughly what's your current portfolio size?" (qualification)
  • DM 2: Deliver tool link. "Your personalised strategy will be ready in about 3 minutes."
  • DM 3 (2 hours later): "Did you get a chance to check your results? What stood out to you?"
  • DM 4 (24 hours): "Based on your profile, you'd be a great fit for a portfolio review call with our team. Want me to send the link?"
  • If yes -> send booking link. If no response -> enter Customer.io email sequence.
  • Collect: name, email, portfolio size estimate, current digital asset exposure (Y/N)

2. Portfolio Protection Scorecard

SCORECARD
What It Is

Scores the user's portfolio on 5 dimensions: inflation protection, diversification, liquidity, tax efficiency, and digital asset allocation. Returns a score out of 10 with a summary of where they're strong and where they're exposed.

Hook (Post Copy)

"Most HNW investors think their portfolio is protected from inflation. Most are wrong. Comment SCORECARD and I'll send you our portfolio protection scorecard."

Knowledge Gap Created

"I scored 4/10 on portfolio protection. I can see the gaps but I don't know how to fix them."

Platinum Bridge

"Platinum members get each gap identified, addressed, and monitored with our team's ongoing support."

Claims Separation

Magnet: "See where you stand." Platinum: "We'll close every gap."

ManyChat Flow (Tim Build Spec)
  • Trigger: Comment "SCORECARD" on any post OR DM keyword
  • DM 1: "Want to see how protected your portfolio really is? Takes 60 seconds. What's your main asset class right now? (property / shares / cash / mix)"
  • DM 2: Deliver scorecard tool link. "Your score will be ready in about a minute."
  • DM 3 (2 hours later): "What score did you get? Most investors land between 3-5/10. Our Platinum members average 8+."
  • DM 4 (24 hours): "Want to know exactly how to improve your score? Book a 15-min portfolio review."
  • Collect: name, email, main asset class, self-reported portfolio range

3. Digital Asset Allocation Calculator

CALCULATOR
What It Is

Answers the #1 question HNW investors have: "How much of my portfolio should be in digital assets?" Based on risk tolerance, time horizon, and current allocation. Gives a percentage range recommendation.

Hook (Post Copy)

"How much of my portfolio should be in digital assets? We built a calculator that answers it in 60 seconds. Comment CALCULATOR."

Knowledge Gap Created

"I now know 5-10% allocation makes sense, but I don't know WHICH digital assets, or HOW to actually structure this."

Platinum Bridge

"Platinum shows you which assets, when to enter, how to structure, and manages your allocation with our framework."

Claims Separation

Magnet: "See how much." Platinum: "We'll show you exactly what and how."

ManyChat Flow (Tim Build Spec)
  • Trigger: Comment "CALCULATOR" on any post OR DM keyword
  • DM 1: "Great question! Let me send you our allocation calculator. Quick one first: are you currently invested in any digital assets?" (Y/N qualification)
  • DM 2: Deliver calculator link. "Takes about 60 seconds. You'll get a recommended allocation range."
  • DM 3 (2 hours later): "What range did the calculator suggest? Most investors with your profile land around 5-10%."
  • DM 4 (24 hours): "Knowing HOW MUCH is step one. Knowing WHICH assets and WHEN to enter is what separates the top performers. Want a quick call to map it out?"
  • Collect: name, email, current DA exposure (Y/N), portfolio size range

Universal Flow Architecture

Trigger Setup
  • Comment automation: detect keyword in IG comments
  • DM keyword: detect keyword in IG DMs
  • Story reply: detect keyword in story replies
  • Multiple spellings: STRATEGY, strategy, Strategy, etc.
Data Collection
  • Name (from IG profile or asked)
  • Email (required for Customer.io handoff)
  • Portfolio size range (qualification)
  • Current DA exposure Y/N (segmentation)
  • Location AU/NZ/other (for TAX flow)
Integration Requirements
  • Push email + data to Customer.io (triggers email sequence)
  • Push qualified leads to Pipedrive (setter pipeline)
  • Tag by keyword (STRATEGY/SCORECARD/etc.) for segmentation
  • Tag by qualification level (portfolio $500K+, $200K-500K, under $200K)
  • Exclude active Platinum members (check against Customer.io list)
  • Speed-to-lead alert: notify setter within 5 minutes for $500K+ qualified leads
