Section 03

Typeform — Full Build Specification

Setup First

Before adding questions: go to Logic → Variables → + Add variable. Name it score, type Number, default value 0. This is the scoring engine. Do this before building any questions.

Q1 — Your Starting Point

QUALIFIER — Not scored
Where are you right now with digital assets?
There's no right answer here. This helps us tailor your blueprint to where you actually are, not where you think you should be.
  • A Researching only — I haven't made any moves yet
  • B Made some purchases but I don't have a clear strategy
  • C Have active positions but I'm making reactive decisions without a real system
  • D I'm experienced — I want a better framework to optimise what I already have

No logic required. Used for personalisation only — CRM tagging.

Q2 — Your Primary Goal

QUALIFIER — Not scored
What's your primary reason for getting serious about digital assets now?
Most people have more than one goal. Pick the one that matters most — it shapes which parts of the framework are most relevant for you.
  • A Protect my wealth from inflation and currency debasement
  • B Build long-term generational wealth for my family
  • C Grow my wealth faster than traditional assets allow
  • D Buy back my time and build genuine financial freedom

No logic required. Used for personalisation + CRM tagging.

Q3 — Capital Outlook

QUALIFIER — Not scored
How much are you looking to deploy into digital assets over the next 12 months?
This is about your intention, not a commitment. It helps us calibrate the scale of the framework you need.
  • A Under $25,000
  • B $25,000 – $100,000
  • C $100,000 – $500,000
  • D $500,000 – $1,000,000
  • E $1,000,000+

GHL automation: Tag contacts based on this answer. C/D/E = high-value lead, notify sales.

Q4 — Investment Thesis

SCORED — 1 to 4 points
How would you describe your current view on why Bitcoin and digital assets may belong in a wealth portfolio?
Most people at this stage have done the research but haven't been able to articulate a clear view. That's normal — this is exactly what we help with.
  • A Still researching — I haven't formed a clear view yet +1 pt
  • B I have a general sense but can't articulate it clearly +2 pts
  • C I understand the macro case but haven't committed to a written thesis +3 pts
  • D I have a clear, written thesis I'm confident in +4 pts

Typeform logic: Add 4 conditions after this question — one per answer. If A selected → score = score + 1. If B → score + 2. If C → score + 3. If D → score + 4.

Q5 — Information Sources

SCORED — 1 to 4 points
How do you currently stay informed about digital assets?
Information overload is the second biggest pain point we see. The problem isn't access to information — it's filtering what actually matters.
  • A Social media, YouTube, influencers — wherever the news is +1 pt
  • B Mix of news and some research — hard to filter signal from noise +2 pts
  • C A small number of trusted sources, trying to build my own view +3 pts
  • D On-chain data and primary research — I have a clear filter for what I act on +4 pts

Typeform logic: Same pattern — 4 conditions, score + 1/2/3/4.

Q6 — Risk Framework

SCORED — 1 to 4 points
Do you have written rules for your digital asset allocation and what you'd do in a major drawdown?
Written rules sound formal, but they're the difference between a strategy and a guess. Most high-net-worth investors we work with start without them.
  • A No — I'd work it out when the time comes +1 pt
  • B Rough ideas, nothing written down +2 pts
  • C Some guidelines but not formalised +3 pts
  • D Written allocation rules and a clear drawdown plan +4 pts

Q7 — Time Available

SCORED — 1 to 4 points
How much time can you realistically dedicate to managing a digital asset portfolio each month?
Be honest here. The right strategy depends on how much time you actually have, not how much you think you should have.
  • A Very little — I need something I can almost set and forget +1 pt
  • B A few hours a month if it's clearly directed +2 pts
  • C A few hours a week — I want to stay actively informed +3 pts
  • D As much as needed — I'm prepared to put in the work +4 pts

Q8 — Exit Strategy

SCORED — 1 to 4 points
Do you have a written plan for when and how you'd exit your digital asset positions?
An entry without an exit isn't a strategy. This is the question that separates investors from speculators — and the most common gap we see.
  • A No — I'd decide when the time comes +1 pt
  • B Rough idea but nothing specific or written +2 pts
  • C Some targets but haven't thought through all scenarios +3 pts
  • D Written sell strategy with specific triggers and targets +4 pts

Q9 — Advanced Strategies

SCORED — 1 to 4 points
How familiar are you with using your digital assets to generate income — through lending, yield, or tax-efficient structures?
Yield and tax optimisation are two of the most underutilised advantages available to serious digital asset investors.
  • A Not familiar at all — I didn't know this was possible +1 pt
  • B I've heard of it but don't know how to approach it safely +2 pts
  • C I understand the basics but haven't set anything up yet +3 pts
  • D I actively use lending/yield strategies and have a tax structure in place +4 pts

  • 1 Short Text field: First name (required)

Typeform Ending — Redirect URL

Go to Endings → Redirect to a URL and set:
https://[YOUR-GHL-DOMAIN]/results?score={{score}}&goal={{field:Q2_answer}}
The {{score}} variable passes their total score to the results page so it shows their personalised archetype and message. Replace [YOUR-GHL-DOMAIN] with your GHL funnel domain.

Typeform → GHL Integration

  1. In Typeform: Connect → GoHighLevel → Authorize
  2. Select your Collective Shift sub-account
  3. Map fields: First Name → First Name | Email → Email | Phone → Phone Number
  4. Optional: map Q3 (Capital Outlook) to a GHL custom field for lead scoring
  5. Save — every submission now creates/updates a contact in GHL
Section 04

Scoring System

Q1-Q3 are unscored qualifiers. Q4-Q9 are scored 1-4 points each. Maximum score: 24 points.

Score: 6 – 10

Cautious Observer

Strong on caution. Gap: no structured entry framework yet.

"You're asking the right questions. Here's the framework to act on them."

Score: 11 – 15

Hesitant First Allocator

Ready to move. Gap: missing conviction and process.

"The intent is there. The structure isn't yet."

Score: 16 – 20

Reactive Dabbler

Has experience. Gap: reactive decisions, no system.

"You've started. Now it's time to build a strategy around what you have."

Score: 21 – 24

Structured Allocator

Strong foundation. Gap: optimising and protecting gains.

"The framework is working. The next level is refinement and risk protection."

Note on Results Page Personalisation

The results page receives the score via the URL parameter ?score=XX. GHL custom JavaScript (or a conditional section) reads this and shows the matching archetype block. If GHL's page builder doesn't support custom JS easily, a simpler option is to create 4 separate results pages in GHL (one per archetype) and use Typeform Logic to redirect to the correct URL based on the final score range.

Page 2 — Results Page Structure

Section A — Score Card (above fold)

  • Eyebrow: "Your Assessment Results"
  • Personalised headline based on score: e.g. "Here's where you stand, [First Name]."
  • Score ring/graphic: Show score out of 24 (e.g. animated circle or bold number)
  • Archetype label: e.g. "Reactive Dabbler" — bold, orange
  • 1 paragraph: Personalised message matching their archetype (copy above)
  • CTA button (orange): Book Your Free Digital Asset Strategy Call →

Section B — The Mirror (pain section)

  • Heading: "Most Digital Asset Investors Make the Same Three Mistakes"
  • Mistake 1: No exit strategy — they watch gains evaporate because they never decided when to sell
  • Mistake 2: Information overload — they follow too many voices and end up paralysed or reactive
  • Mistake 3: Emotional allocation — they buy when confident and sell when fearful, the exact opposite of what works
  • Transition line: "The 8-step framework below is designed to eliminate all three."

Section C — 8-Step Blueprint (core content)

  • Step 1: Build Your Macro Thesis — understand why Bitcoin belongs in a wealth portfolio before allocating
  • Step 2: Filter Your Information Sources — create a trusted research stack and eliminate the noise
  • Step 3: Define Your Entry Strategy — set written criteria for when and how you deploy capital
  • Step 4: Build Your Portfolio Allocation — position sizing, asset selection, and rebalancing rules
  • Step 5: Secure Your Assets — hardware wallets, custody options, and backup protocols
  • Step 6: Generate Yield — staking, lending, and DeFi strategies that don't require active trading
  • Step 7: Manage Your Risk — written drawdown rules, position limits, and stop scenarios
  • Step 8: Build Your Exit Strategy — written sell targets, profit-taking levels, and tax-efficient exit structures

Section D — Strategy Call CTA (bottom)

  • Heading: "Want Help Implementing This With Your Portfolio?"
  • Subtext: "Book a free 20-minute Digital Asset Strategy Call. We'll walk through your assessment results, identify the gaps, and show you what a structured approach looks like for your specific situation. No hard sell. Educational session."
  • CTA button (orange): Book Your Free Strategy Call →
  • Link to: GHL calendar booking page
  • Fine print: "Not financial advice. Educational session only. Collective Shift Pty Ltd ABN 78 650 977 325."
Section 06

GHL Automation — Post Submission

  1. Trigger: Typeform submission received (new contact created) Fires on every completed Typeform submission.
  2. Tag contact based on Q3 — Capital Outlook Under $25K → tag: lead-early-stage
    $25K–$100K → tag: lead-mid-tier
    $100K+ → tag: lead-platinum-qualified + notify sales team immediately
  3. Add to nurture email sequence Start 5-email nurture sequence: framework education content, social proof, then strategy call invite on email 5.
  4. If Q3 = $100K+ — Internal notification Send Slack/email alert to sales team: "New high-value lead — [Name], [Email], Capital: $100K+, Score: [score]"
  5. If contact doesn't book call within 48 hours — Follow-up SMS SMS: "Hi [First Name], your Digital Asset Readiness Score is ready. Your personalised blueprint is here: [link]. Want to walk through it together? [book link]"
