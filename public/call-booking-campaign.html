5. Optimization & Best Practices

Subject Line A/B Testing Plan

  • Test one variable at a time. Subject line A vs B, not subject + preview text + send time simultaneously.
  • Sample size: Send variant A to 15% of the segment, variant B to 15%, wait 2 hours, send winner to remaining 70%.
  • What to test: Market data vs curiosity ("$53 billion and counting" vs "Something just happened in digital assets"). Specific scarcity vs urgency angle ("14 spots left" vs "80 days. No strategy. That's a problem.").
  • Measure: Open rate for subject lines. Click rate for body copy variations. Book rate for CTA placement.
  • Track in Customer.io: Tag each campaign step. Use UTM parameters on the calendar link: ?utm_source=email&utm_medium=campaign&utm_campaign=4day-sessions-v2&utm_content=email1a

Reply Handling

  • Team replies to: Anyone who mentions a specific portfolio size ($100K+), anyone who asks a thoughtful question, anyone who says "I've been following you for X years", anyone who sounds like a qualified Platinum prospect.
  • Team handles: Scheduling questions, technical issues with the calendar link, general "tell me more" responses (send to VSL page), unsubscribe requests.
  • Template for team: "Hey [NAME], thanks for the reply. I've forwarded this to the team. In the meantime, here's the link to book your session: [CALENDAR_LINK]. Talk soon."

Overbooked? Waitlist Strategy

  • If all 25 spots fill before Day 3, send a "Sold Out" email immediately to the full list.
  • Include a waitlist link: "All spots are taken. But if someone cancels or we add a few extra slots, I want to make sure you're first in line. Join the waitlist: [WAITLIST_LINK]"
  • This is actually the best outcome. It validates the scarcity and creates demand for the next round.
  • Add 3-5 overflow spots the next week for waitlist contacts. Send them a "A spot opened up" email.

Running This Quarterly Without Fatigue

  • Rotate the market angle: Q2 = "EOFY + Institutional Wave." Q3 = "Mid-Year Portfolio Check + Halving Anniversary." Q4 = "Regulation Clarity + Year-End Planning." Q1 = "New Year + Fresh Market Data."
  • Rotate the bonus: Q2 = Blueprint + EOFY Tax Guide. Q3 = Blueprint + Exit Strategy Template. Q4 = Blueprint + Market Brief. Q1 = Blueprint + Portfolio Review.
  • Never run the same copy twice. Refresh all 5 steps each quarter. Same structure, new market data and testimonials.
  • Rest period: Minimum 6 weeks between strategy session campaigns. Run value-only content emails in between.
  • Exclude recent participants: Anyone who booked a session in the last campaign should not receive the next one. They are either a client or in the nurture pipeline.
