▎Brand Voice Rules

✅ Do's
✓Say "digital assets" not "crypto" (except "cryptocurrency" in proper context)
✓Always capitalise "Bitcoin" as a proper noun
✓Tone: Direct, authentic, educator. Speak like a trusted mentor
✓Lead with education and value first
✓Use data and evidence to back claims
✓Be transparent about risks and uncertainty
✓Use short sentences and clear language
✓Show the human side - Ben's real experience
❌ Don'ts
✗No em dashes (—) ever. They look like AI. Use regular dashes, full stops, or rewrite
✗Never be salesy or over-produced
✗No hype language ("to the moon", "guaranteed returns", "get rich quick")
✗No financial advice language (we educate, we don't advise)
✗Don't use "crypto" as shorthand. It's "Bitcoin" or "digital assets"
✗No clickbait or sensationalist headlines
✗Don't talk down to the audience. Respect their intelligence
✗No jargon without explanation. If you use a term, explain it
