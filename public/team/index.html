🗓️ The 90-Day Member Journey

Days 1–30: Foundation

Complete L1 Steps 1–4. Attend weekly Foundation calls. Book 1:1 onboarding session. Set up exchange + wallet.

Days 31–60: Build

Complete L1 Steps 5–8. Start portfolio allocation. Attend strategy calls. Begin L2 if confident.

Days 61–90: Advance

Complete L2 track. Refine strategy with 1:1 coaching. Use 8-Step Strategy Workbook. Full investment confidence.

YouTube Strategy — Jeremy Haynes 90/10

Our YouTube content follows the Jeremy Haynes 90/10 framework:

90%
Value-First Content

Education, market analysis, tutorials, how-to guides. Build trust and authority. Every video should teach something actionable.

10%
Promotional Content

Membership CTAs, testimonials, product announcements. Soft sell — always wrapped in value. Never hard-pitch.

🗣️ Brand Voice Rules

✅ We ARE
  • Educator-first — teach, don't sell
  • Data-driven — back claims with evidence
  • Honest about risk — crypto is volatile
  • Long-term focused — no day trading hype
  • Professional but approachable
  • Bitcoin-native with altcoin awareness
❌ We are NOT
  • Moon boys — no "to the moon" hype
  • Financial advisors — education only
  • Shilling specific coins for clout
  • Doom-and-gloom FUD spreaders
  • Get-rich-quick promoters
  • Trading signal groups
