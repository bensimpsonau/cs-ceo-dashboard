Jeremy Haynes' 20 marketing skills framework has been analysed and adapted specifically for Collective Shift's current position: $7.3M ARR, $180K monthly renewal target, a lean team post-restructure, and a paid acquisition machine running at breakeven. The Hidden Sell Content Formula

0-30s
Hook: Problem or insight the ICP cares about. Not "I'm going to teach you X" but "Here's what's happening with [market event]."
30s-80%
Value: Genuine education. Give away real insight. The kind of thing people would pay $500 for at a conference.
80-90%
Gap: Naturally introduce the gap between what they learned and what they need to implement it.
90-95%
Bridge: "That's exactly what we help our members figure out."
Final 5%
CTA: Soft. "Link in the description if you want to learn more about how we work with investors like you."
Quick Wins This Week: (1) Add UTM parameters to VSL links in last 10 YouTube descriptions. (2) Review top 5 videos and document CTA usage.
