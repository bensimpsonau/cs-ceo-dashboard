00 / Recording Brief

Pre-Production Checklist

Everything Ben needs to know before hitting record. Wardrobe, location, key talking points to memorise, slides to prepare, and things to avoid saying on camera.

👔 What to Wear
• Clean, dark-coloured button-up shirt (navy or charcoal) — no logos
• No tie — approachable but professional
• Avoid stripes, patterns, and bright whites (camera flicker)
• Ensure shirt is pressed and wrinkle-free
• Simple watch is fine — no flashy jewellery
📍 Where to Record
• Clean, professional background — office or neutral backdrop
• Good natural lighting from the front or professional lighting kit
• Quiet room, no echo (soft furnishings help)
• Camera at eye level, head and shoulders framing
• Lapel mic or shotgun mic — no built-in camera mic
🧠 Key Points to Memorise

Don't memorise the script word-for-word. Memorise these anchor points and speak naturally around them.

Opening Hook
The 60/40 portfolio is broken. Sophisticated investors are quietly adding a new asset class. Not speculation — protection.
The Big Number
$1.2 trillion in institutional capital has flowed into digital assets since 2024. BlackRock, Fidelity, sovereign wealth funds.
The Framework
5-layer Wealth Protection Model: Allocation → Vehicle → Custody → Tax Structure → Exit Rules
The CTA
Book a free 30-minute portfolio review. We'll map this to your situation. Link below.
📊 Slides Needed
1. Title slide — "How Sophisticated Investors Are Allocating to Digital Assets"
2. The 60/40 breakdown chart (inflation-adjusted returns since 2020)
3. Institutional adoption timeline (BlackRock IBIT, Fidelity FBTC, sovereign funds)
4. Correlation matrix — BTC vs stocks vs bonds vs real estate
5. The 5-Layer Wealth Protection Model diagram
6. Vehicle comparison table (ETF vs direct vs IRA)
7. Member case study results (anonymised)
8. CTA slide — "Book Your Free Portfolio Review"
🚫 What NOT to Say
Hard rules — never say these on camera:

• No specific return promises ("you'll make X%")
• No "crypto" — always say "digital assets" or "Bitcoin"
• No "guaranteed" or "risk-free" — ever
• No bashing traditional finance advisors — position as "different lens, not wrong"
• No urgency faking ("only 3 spots left") — be genuine
• No "get rich quick" framing — this is wealth protection
• No specific tax advice — "consult your tax professional" for anything specific
• No mention of specific altcoins — Bitcoin and digital assets only
