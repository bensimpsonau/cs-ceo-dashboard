06

Product Implications for Travis (CGO)

  • 📺
    YouTube = #1 acquisition channel for HNW beginners — Dee's exact journey (YouTube search → competitors → CS → Platinum) is validated and repeatable. Our YouTube strategy is the funnel.
  • 🎯
    Core messaging must be: "Protection, Preservation, Growth" — NOT gains, returns, or moon. HNW beginners are driven by fear of loss and macroeconomic anxiety, not greed.
  • 🏠
    Circle Beginner's Lounge is critical infrastructure — the intimidation barrier is a documented churn risk. Members won't post, won't engage, won't renew if they feel behind everyone else.
  • 🛤️
    2-Track system (L1 / L2) is validated — every member interaction confirms the need for a structured beginner path. Mixing beginners with advanced members is hurting retention.
  • ⏱️
    Pro / Accelerator = 90-day access — this product change creates urgency and a natural upgrade path. Reinforce in onboarding communication.
  • 📋
    Invest Diva's platform structure is worth studying directly — calendar depth, learning paths, and the graduation/certification model are all things we should evaluate for CS's platform roadmap.
  • 📉
    Bear market = best time to acquire new members — reframe all marketing and sales messaging. HNW beginners are more motivated by macroeconomic fear in a downturn, not less.
  • 🤝
    In-person events convert — Harry was acquired via CryptoCon. Dee immediately volunteered for a Toronto event. Live events build trust faster than any digital channel.
  • 🎥
    Short how-to videos are a blocker for deployment — Dee has $100K cash ready but won't deploy without knowing how to set up 2FA, buy safely, and manage security. This content gap is costing member outcomes.
07

Recommendations

  1. Study Invest Diva's platform structure in depth — reverse-engineer the calendar model, learning paths, and graduation/certification system. Identify what's directly applicable to CS's platform roadmap.
  2. Prioritise Circle Beginner's Lounge launch — this is the single highest-leverage product action to reduce intimidation-driven churn among Level 1 members. Timeline should be <30 days.
  3. Create a Level 1 how-to video library (5–10 min modules) — minimum viable set: setting up 2FA, buying digital assets for the first time, self-custody intro, DCA setup. These are blocking member outcomes today.
  4. Anchor YouTube content strategy to "Bloomberg for Digital Assets" positioning — serious, analytical, education-first. This is what differentiates Ben from every other influencer and it's the reason HNW beginners choose CS over competitors.
  5. Explore a Toronto in-person event — Dee volunteered immediately. Canada is a validated HNW acquisition market. A single event could convert a cohort of Dee-profile members.
  6. Proactive outreach to Oct–Dec 2025 cohort before renewal dates — members sitting in drawdowns are the highest churn risk. A personal touchpoint from Ben or the team 30 days before renewal will meaningfully improve retention.
  7. Build a portfolio allocation calculator — the Bitcoin vs altcoins allocation question appears on nearly every call. A self-serve tool would reduce repetitive 1-on-1 content time and create a high-value acquisition asset for YouTube/landing pages.
