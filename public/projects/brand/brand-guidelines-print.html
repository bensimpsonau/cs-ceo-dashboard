Section 01

Brand Identity

Who we are, what we stand for, and who we serve.

Mission

Collective Shift empowers Bitcoin and digital asset investors with professional-grade, systematic intelligence — the research, tools, and community that institutional investors have always had, now accessible to the individual.

Brand Positioning

Collective Shift is a long-term, research-driven membership for serious investors. Bitcoin-native. No affiliates. No advertising. No token promotions. Trust-based, member-funded.

Section 07

Photography & Imagery

Premium wealth management aesthetic — not crypto culture.

Direction

Professional, warm, elevated. Think private wealth advisory — not crypto tech startup.

✅ Use
  • Professional, warm lighting
  • Clean, uncluttered compositions
  • Wealth management aesthetic
  • Real team photos
  • Charts / data visualisations
  • White / light backgrounds for content
❌ Avoid
  • Crypto bro / hype aesthetics
  • Neon lights, dark futuristic scenes
  • Generic stock photos
  • Gaming / tech startup vibes
  • Loud, flashy graphics
  • Overly dark compositions
Section 08

Voice & Tone

How Collective Shift sounds — every channel, every touchpoint.

Writing Principles

Ben is a peer investor — not a guru. Direct, conviction-driven, warm authority. Data-backed, never hype-driven.

Brand Language

✅ Use
  • "Cut through the noise"
  • "Systematic intelligence"
  • "Generational wealth"
  • "Financial sovereignty"
  • "Professional-grade"
  • "No affiliates. No advertising."
  • "By Application Only"
  • "Done-for-you research"
❌ Never
  • "To the moon" / crypto slang
  • "Get rich quick"
  • "Guaranteed returns"
  • "We recommend" (legal risk)
  • "Sign up now!" / "Buy today!"
  • "Game-changer" / "World-class"
  • "Unlock your potential"
  • Any FOMO / urgency theatre

Voice Examples

✅ On-Brand
Email: "Why 74% of investors have no exit strategy"
Social: "The institutional window is closing."
CTA: "Apply for Platinum"
Proof: "350 HNW members, $100K–$35M+"
❌ Off-Brand
Email: "🚀 Don't miss this HUGE opportunity!!!"
Social: "Bitcoin to $1M. Are you ready??"
CTA: "Sign up now!"
Proof: "Thousands of happy customers!"
Section 09

Presentation Standards

Standing design orders for all decks and visual materials.

Design Direction

White backgrounds for content. Dark for hero moments only. Light themes = finance, trust, comfort. Dark themes = tech, gaming, discomfort for the ICP.

Slide Backgrounds

TypeBackgroundNotes
Content#FFFFFF / #F9F9F9Default — light mode
Hero / Title#02000DDark — opening moments only
Section intro#1B1925Dark dividers
CTA#FC5C03Blaze Orange — sparingly

Presentation Rules

✅ Do
  • Bold declarative statements
  • One metric per data callout
  • Max 3 key points per slide
  • White text on orange dividers
  • Charts as focal points, full-width
  • White backgrounds for content
❌ Don't
  • Bullet point overload
  • Multiple competing charts
  • Black text on orange (never)
  • Text below 14px
  • Dark backgrounds for everything
  • Crowded, cluttered layouts
