Three Components. One Document. Done.

Target-Based

Define price targets → sell defined % at each. Example: 10% at 2×, 20% at 3×, 30% at 5×

Time-Based

Take profits at defined cycle points regardless of price. You do not have to call the top.

Indicator-Based

CS flags on-chain signals: NUPL, MVRV, LTH distribution. Have your plan ready when signals fire.

A sell strategy is not about leaving money on the table. It is about not giving it all back.
