Sell Framework

Three Components. One Written Document. Done.

Target-Based

Define price targets → sell defined % at each.

Example: 10% at 2×, 20% at 3×, 30% at 5×

Time-Based

Take some profits at defined cycle points regardless of price.

You don't have to call the top.

Indicator-Based

CS research flags on-chain signals: NUPL, MVRV, LTH distribution.

Have your plan ready when signals fire.

A sell strategy is not about leaving money on the table. It's about not giving it all back.

Tax Reality

Every Disposal Is a CGT Event.
Every Single One.

Key Facts

  • Disposals include: selling for AUD, crypto swaps, DeFi transactions
  • 12+ month hold → 50% CGT discount
  • Your time horizon affects your tax bill as much as your price target

Record-Keeping

  • ✅ Use Koinly or CryptoTaxCalculator — start now
  • ✅ Every transaction: date, value, fees
  • ✅ Every DeFi interaction — all of it
