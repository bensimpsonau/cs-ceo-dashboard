"The Most Painful Way to Lose Money in Digital Assets Is to Taxes and Poor Structure."

  • Build legal/financial structures that protect your wealth after you've built it
  • Position yourself to engage professional advisors effectively — before you need them
  • CGT events: every trade, swap, DeFi interaction is potentially taxable
"This is not financial advice. This is the framework for getting the right advice at the right time."

"Every Disposal Is a CGT Event. Every Single One."

What counts as a disposal
  • Selling for AUD
  • Digital asset-to-digital asset swaps
  • DeFi transactions
  • Spending digital assets
Key advantage

12+ month hold → 50% CGT discount.

"Your time horizon affects your tax bill as much as your price target."

Record-Keeping Rules
  • Use Koinly or CryptoTaxCalculator — start now, not at tax time
  • Every transaction: date, value at time, fees
  • Every DeFi interaction needs to be recorded
"The ATO has significantly increased its focus on digital assets."

"If You Don't Have This Conversation Before You Have Gains to Protect — You're Paying More Tax Than You Need To."

SMSF
  • Hold Bitcoin inside super
  • 15% tax rate vs. marginal rate
  • Pension phase: potentially 0% on gains
  • Requires SMSF advisor with digital asset experience
Family Trust
  • Distribute gains to lower-tax beneficiaries
  • Estate planning benefits
  • "Know this exists before you sell"
Company Structure
  • 25–30% corporate tax rate
  • Less flexibility for distributions
  • Right for some situations

Not financial advice. Speak to a specialist with digital asset experience.

"Building Wealth and Keeping It Are Two Different Skills."

  • Estate planning must include digital assets — who has the seed phrase? How is it transferred?
  • "I've heard of families losing Bitcoin forever because no one knew where the seed phrase was."
  • Build an access document: sealed letter to family/executor — instructions only, not the seed phrase itself
  • Insurance: check if digital assets are covered under existing policies. Most aren't.
"Wealth preservation is the unglamorous part of this journey. But it's what separates building wealth from keeping it."

"Most Accountants Don't Understand Digital Assets Well Enough. That's a Problem."

4 questions to ask any advisor:

1. "Do you have experience with digital asset CGT and self-custody in Australia?"

2. "Do you understand DeFi taxation?"

3. "Have you helped clients set up SMSF for digital assets?"

4. "Can you give me a client reference in this area?"

If they can't answer confidently — find someone who can. CS connects members with digital asset specialist advisors — ask your CSM.
