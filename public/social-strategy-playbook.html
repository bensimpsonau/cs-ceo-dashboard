01 / Social Strategy Audit

A platform-by-platform assessment of what exists, what is working, what is missing, and what needs to change. Based on ICP data (n=76 survey + ~90 sales calls), the "Silent Thief" 3,266x ROAS ad, validated acquisition path data, and the Jeremy Haynes organic framework.

📸
Instagram
Primary Acquisition Channel
What Exists
Ben's personal brand is the primary account. Instagram drives 25-50% of direct acquisition (confirmed by sales call attribution). Reels and Stories are the main formats. "The Silent Thief" inflation ad achieved 3,266x ROAS from this platform. Content is largely MOFU (mid-funnel education) with some market commentary. Paid ads are running.
What's Working
Ben's authentic personal brand. The follow-for-weeks/months-then-convert path is validated by real member data (Dee's acquisition story). The Silent Thief angle taps directly into ICP fear of invisible wealth erosion. HNW audience responds to calm, data-led education over hype. Instagram-to-masterclass pipeline delivers qualified leads.
What's Missing
No systematic content calendar. No documented comment strategy (the algorithm rewards comments within 60 minutes of posting). Travis and Sarn are not creating content here. No TOFU pre-framing content. No carousel posts (highest save rate for education content). No Reels series (branded recurring format). No hashtag strategy documented. No Stories-to-DM conversion workflow.
Recommended Changes
Deploy all 3 personas (Ben, Travis, Sarn) with differentiated content. Add TOFU layer from the 4 content buckets. Build a carousel template for data-heavy educational posts. Create a "60-minute rule" for comment responses. Implement Stories polls/questions to drive DMs. Add Sarn as the beginner-focused voice. Shift to 50% TOFU / 30% MOFU / 20% BOFU content split.
▶️
YouTube
Primary Trust / Education Channel
What Exists
Ben's YouTube is the validation channel. ICP discovers Ben on Instagram, then goes to YouTube to verify credibility. Long-form education content (15-25 min). Market analysis, Bitcoin case, investing frameworks. Validated by member acquisition data: "Searches YouTube for education, finds Ben, sincerity wins them over."
What's Working
Ben's teaching style is a core differentiator. Long-form builds deep trust with HNW audience who need to verify before committing $15K. The education-first, no-affiliate stance is a major trust signal. YouTube content directly supports the sales call ("I watched your videos for months before calling").
What's Missing
No YouTube Shorts strategy (massive reach opportunity). No Sarn deep-dive content. No Travis solo content. No systematic repurpose workflow (1 long-form should produce 12-18 pieces). No end-screen strategy driving to quiz/lead magnet. No community tab engagement. Upload schedule is inconsistent. No playlists organized by ICP journey stage.
Recommended Changes
1 long-form per persona per week (4 total). Extract 3-5 YouTube Shorts from every long-form. Sarn launches a "Digital Asset 101" deep-dive series. Travis launches "Wealth Architecture" series. Build playlists: "Start Here," "Market Analysis," "Exit Strategy Framework." Every video end-screen drives to quiz. Begin Shorts to capture discovery traffic.
💼
LinkedIn
Professional Trust + Travis Primary
What Exists
Minimal presence. Ben has a profile but LinkedIn is not a priority channel. No consistent posting. No Travis or Matt content despite LinkedIn being their natural habitat.
What's Working
The ICP (business owners, executives, professionals aged 40-59) lives on LinkedIn. Travis's "Goal Architecture" angle and Matt's "Data Angle" are perfectly suited for LinkedIn's professional tone. LinkedIn organic reach for text posts remains strong in 2026.
What's Missing
Everything. No content strategy, no posting cadence, no profile optimization, no comment engagement. Travis (CGO) should own this platform but has zero presence. Matt's research insights are perfectly formatted for LinkedIn but are not being published. No company page strategy. No employee advocacy program.
Recommended Changes
Travis becomes the primary LinkedIn voice. 3-5 text posts per week from Travis. Matt contributes 2-3 data-driven posts per week. Ben cross-posts key authority content. Optimize all profiles with ICP-relevant headlines. Build "Wealth Architecture" series as a LinkedIn-native text format. Comment strategy within 60 minutes on all posts.
🎵
TikTok
Discovery + Reach Engine
What Exists
Limited or no active presence. Some cross-posted content from Instagram but no TikTok-native strategy.
What's Working
TikTok's algorithm favours new accounts with strong hooks. The "Silent Thief" angle and purchasing power data are perfect for TikTok's pattern-interrupt format. Finance/education content is growing rapidly on TikTok with an increasingly older demographic (35-55 is the fastest growing segment).
What's Missing
No dedicated TikTok content. No platform-native formatting (vertical, fast cuts, text overlays, trending audio). No consistent posting. No bio link to quiz. No comment engagement strategy.
Recommended Changes
Repurpose IG Reels as TikTok-native clips (re-edit, do not just cross-post). Ben and Sarn as primary TikTok personas. 3-5 clips per week. Focus on Bucket 1 (Fiat Illusion) and Bucket 4 (Institutional Front-Run) for maximum pattern interrupt. Add trending audio where appropriate. Bio link to quiz.

X / Twitter
Real-Time Commentary + Threads
What Exists
Ben has a presence but posting is inconsistent. Some market commentary and Bitcoin analysis.
What's Working
Digital asset / Bitcoin community is deeply active on X. Real-time market commentary gets high engagement. Thread format is ideal for the data-heavy arguments in our 4 content buckets. X drives conversations that YouTube and Instagram cannot (debate, hot takes, breaking news).
What's Missing
No consistent posting cadence. No thread strategy (threads are the highest-engagement format on X). No Matt contributions (his data insights are perfect for X). No real-time market commentary workflow. No quote-tweet engagement strategy. No pinned tweet driving to quiz.
Recommended Changes
Ben and Matt as primary X voices. 3-5 posts/threads per week each. Convert YouTube key moments into X threads. Pin a high-performing thread that drives to quiz. Matt publishes "one chart that matters" weekly. Real-time commentary during major market moves. Reply to high-follower accounts in the space for visibility.

Facebook
Paid Ads + Retargeting
What Exists
Facebook is primarily a paid ads channel (Meta Ads Manager serves both IG and FB). Some organic content cross-posted from Instagram. "The Silent Thief" ad runs on Facebook with strong ROAS.
What's Working
Paid ads. The ICP demographic (40-59) over-indexes on Facebook vs other platforms. The Silent Thief ad performs well on both IG and FB placements. Retargeting warm audiences (video viewers, site visitors) is effective.
What's Missing
No organic strategy (and that is probably fine). No Facebook Group community play. No event/webinar promotion workflow.
Recommended Changes
Keep Facebook as a paid-first platform. Cross-post top-performing Instagram content for organic reach. Use Facebook Events for masterclass/webinar promotion. Consider a private Facebook Group as a low-friction community entry point for warm leads (not a priority in Month 1-2). Focus energy on Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, and TikTok for organic.

Podcast (The Wealth Shift)
Conviction Layer + Long-Form Trust
What Exists
The Wealth Shift Podcast is planned (House of Brands strategy). Designed as the conviction layer: where passive interest hardens into genuine conviction before a discovery call. Four formats: Member Story, Expert Interview, Team Panel, Travis Solo.
What's Working
The concept is strong. Our ICP consumes long-form audio (commuters, FIFO workers, business owners in the car). Podcast guests bring cross-promotion opportunities. Real member stories are the most powerful trust-builder.
What's Missing
Not yet launched. No distribution strategy across Spotify, Apple Podcasts, YouTube Podcasts. No clip strategy for social promotion. No guest pipeline built. No recording schedule set.
Recommended Changes
Launch in Month 2 (May 2026). Start with bi-weekly episodes. Film video versions for YouTube. Extract 5-8 clips per episode for social distribution. Build a guest pipeline: digital asset fund managers, HNW members willing to share their story, macro economists. Each episode ends with quiz CTA.
02 / Platform-by-Platform Playbooks

Complete playbook card for each platform. Primary persona, content formats, funnel split, frequency, timing, hooks, comments, hashtags, collaborations, and KPIs. Designed for the AU HNW audience (40-59, $500K-$2M+ net worth, time-poor professionals).

📸
Instagram Playbook
Primary Acquisition Channel
Personas
Ben (Primary) Sarn (Beginner) Travis (Operator)
Formats
Reels (60-90 sec talking head + text overlay), Carousels (data breakdowns, 7-10 slides), Stories (polls, Q&A, behind-the-scenes), Static posts (quote cards with data)
Funnel Split
50% TOFU 30% MOFU 20% BOFU
TOFU: Silent Thief angle, purchasing power data, institutional moves. MOFU: Framework previews, market analysis. BOFU: Member stories, Platinum previews, masterclass CTAs.
Frequency
5 Reels/week (Ben 3, Sarn 1, Travis 1) + 2 Carousels/week + Daily Stories
Best Times
7:00-9:00 AM AEST (professionals checking before work) and 7:00-9:00 PM AEST (post-dinner browse). Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday. Reels: mornings. Carousels: evenings (higher save rate when people have time to read).
Hook Formula
Pattern Interrupt + Data + Open Loop
First 3 seconds: Bold claim with no context. Text overlay matching spoken word. No music intro, no logo bumper. Example: "Your savings lost 25% of their purchasing power in the last 5 years." Then: body with supporting data. Then: open loop CTA driving to quiz or profile link.
Comments
Respond within 60 minutes of posting (algorithm boost window). Every response should either educate, ask a deeper question, or acknowledge + soft CTA. Pin the best educational comment. Never ignore objection comments. See Section 6 for full framework.
Hashtags
3-5 per post (2026 best practice: fewer, more targeted). Mix of: Niche (#DigitalAssetEducation #BitcoinInvesting #WealthProtection) + Audience (#HighNetWorth #WealthBuilder #AustralianInvestor) + Topic (#InflationHedge #PortfolioStrategy #GenerationalWealth). Avoid generic high-volume tags like #investing or #money. No hashtags in caption body. Place in first comment or at end.
Collabs
IG Collab posts with Australian finance educators. Guest Reel appearances from Platinum members (with permission). Cross-promote Wealth Shift Podcast clips. Partner with complementary brands (property investment educators, SMSF advisors).
KPIs
Reach per Reel (target: 10K+ avg). Saves per carousel (target: 100+). Profile visits from content (target: 500+/week). Link clicks to quiz (target: 50+/week). DM conversations started. Follower growth rate (target: 2-3%/month).

Guest appearances on other YouTube channels (macro finance, property investing, FIRE movement). Interview institutional digital asset investors. Cross-channel with podcasters in the finance education space. Collaborative videos with complementary Australian educators.
KPIs
Average view duration (target: 50%+ for long-form). Subscriber growth (target: 500+/month). Click-through rate on end-screens (target: 5%+). Shorts views (target: 50K+ avg). Comments per video (target: 50+). Link clicks to quiz from description.

06 / Comment Strategy Framework

The comment section is where trust converts to action. Every comment is an opportunity to deepen the relationship, address objections, and move the commenter one stage forward. The algorithm rewards early engagement. This framework documents exactly how to handle every comment type.

The 60-Minute Rule
Every platform's algorithm rewards engagement within the first 60 minutes of posting. This is non-negotiable. The person who posted (Ben, Travis, Sarn) or a designated team member must be available for 60 minutes after every post to respond to comments. This is not optional. It is the single highest-ROI activity on social media.
Instagram
Reply to every comment in first 60 min. Heart all comments. Reply with questions to deepen.
YouTube
Reply to first 10 comments. Pin best comment with quiz link. Heart all constructive comments.
LinkedIn
Reply to every comment. LinkedIn rewards conversation depth. Ask follow-up questions.
TikTok / X
Reply within 60 min. TikTok: use "reply with video" for high-engagement comments. X: Quote-tweet insightful replies.
Response Types by Comment Category
Comment Type Example Response Strategy Goal
Positive / Agreement "This is exactly what I needed to hear" Acknowledge + deepen: "Glad it resonated. What is the biggest thing holding you back from taking the first step?" Builds a conversation. If they reply, DM them. DM Conversion
Question / Curiosity "How do I actually start?" Educational reply (2-3 sentences max). End with: "I built a free guide that walks through exactly this. Link in bio." Never write an essay in comments. Quiz / Guide CTA
Objection / Skepticism "Digital assets are too volatile / too risky" Validate first: "I get that. Most people feel that way." Troll / Negative "Scam" / "Ponzi scheme" Do not engage directly. Do not delete (unless abusive). Let the community respond. If it is a genuine concern disguised as aggression, reply once with data: "BlackRock does not build $54B funds for Ponzi schemes." Then disengage. One reply maximum. Disengage

Pin Strategy
Pin the comment that best reinforces the video/post message. Ideal pinned comments:
Self-authored pin: "If this resonated, I built a free wealth assessment that shows you the gap in your current strategy. Link in bio." Pin this to every post if no better organic comment exists.
Social proof pin: If a commenter shares a positive result or agrees powerfully, pin their comment. "This. Exactly why I joined Platinum." Social proof from a real person beats any self-promotion.
Controversy pin: If a thoughtful objection generates good debate, pin it and reply underneath. Shows confidence and transparency. "Great question. Here is the data..." Builds trust more than hiding objections.
