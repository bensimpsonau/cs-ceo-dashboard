2. Lead Magnet Funnel v3.0 - Complete Architecture

The lead magnet system uses the Taki Moore framework: complete solution to a TINY problem, deliver a result in under 3 minutes, "steal this and get this result now" format. Each micro magnet maps directly to a specific step of the 8-step Wealth Protection Blueprint.

The Problem We're Solving

Our ICP is a 40-59 year old business owner or senior professional with $500K-$2M+ net worth. They have significant wealth in traditional assets (property, stocks, super) but no coherent digital asset strategy.

3.7/10Research Confidence
3.2/10Support Network
"Flying blind, need some help." "I can't be with the techno bro. And I can't be with the old world solid bankers."

Three-Layer Architecture

L1

Layer 1: Micro Magnets (Free, 3-Minute Result)

One per content pillar. Solves a tiny, specific problem. Each maps to a specific Blueprint step.

L2

Layer 2: Blueprint Assessment (LIVE - Do Not Change)

9-question diagnostic. Digital Asset Readiness Score out of 24. 4 archetypes. Email gate. Delivers the 8-chapter Blueprint Guide.

L3

Layer 3: Value Ladder

The 4 Micro Magnets

Micro Magnet 1: PROTECT Pillar

Blueprint Step 07 - Tax

Title: "Steal my 3-step tax strategy that saved members $47K last financial year"

Format: 1-page decision tree (PDF or interactive). Three yes/no questions:

  1. Should you be lending against your Bitcoin instead of selling? (No taxable event)
  2. Should your digital assets be held personally or through a structure (trust, company, SMSF)?
  3. The one tax rule most people break without knowing (every swap between assets is a CGT event)

Tiny problem solved: "Am I structured properly, or am I about to get destroyed at tax time?"

ManyChat keywords: PROTECT TAX RISK

Bridge to Layer 2: "Now you know how to protect what you have. But do you actually have a strategy for what to build? Take the 2-minute Digital Asset Readiness Assessment to find out where you stand across all 8 areas."

Micro Magnet 2: STRATEGY Pillar

Blueprint Step 04 - Risk Frameworks

Title: "My Bitcoin allocation calculator - get your number in 60 seconds"

Format: Simple calculator (interactive web page or GPT). Input: total investable assets, risk tolerance, time horizon. Output: suggested allocation percentage.

Tiny problem solved: "How much of my money should actually go into digital assets?"

ManyChat keywords: STRATEGY ALLOCATE

Bridge to Layer 2: "You've got your allocation number. But allocation without a framework is just gambling. The assessment scores you across all 8 areas of the Wealth Protection Blueprint - not just allocation, but exit strategy, tax, security, and more."

Micro Magnet 3: CONFIDENCE Pillar

Blueprint Steps 04, 06, 08

Title: "The 5-minute portfolio risk audit our Platinum members use quarterly"

Format: 1-page checklist (PDF). 7 yes/no questions:

  1. Exchange risk - are your assets on a regulated exchange?
  2. Custody - do you control your keys?
  3. Concentration risk - is more than 40% in one asset?
  4. Stablecoin exposure - which stablecoins and why it matters
  5. Lending protocol risk
  6. Insurance/backup access
  7. Exit triggers - do you have written sell rules?

ManyChat keywords: CONFIDENCE AUDIT

Bridge to Layer 2: "You've audited your risk. But risk management is just Step 6 of the 8-step Wealth Protection Blueprint. The assessment covers all 8 - including the one area where most people score lowest: exit strategy."

Micro Magnet 4: RESULTS Pillar

All Blueprint Steps

Title: "See how 1,400+ professionals scored on the Digital Asset Readiness Assessment"

Format: 1-page infographic showing score distribution, most common archetype (Hesitant First Allocator), #1 gap area (exit strategy / Step 08), and what the top 10% do differently.

ManyChat keywords: RESULTS SCORE

Bridge to Layer 2: "Want to see exactly where you fall? Take the same 2-minute assessment 1,400+ professionals have already completed."
