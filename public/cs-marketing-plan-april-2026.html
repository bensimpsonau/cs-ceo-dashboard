🔴 Urgent — Deploy This Week

#27 Pixel Conditioning

Urgent

What it is: Train Meta's algorithm to find your ideal buyer by stating the price upfront. Unqualified people bounce and tell Meta "not me." Qualified buyers engage and tell Meta "more like me."

Why CS needs this now

If $14,999 isn't visible in the VSL and landing page, every click from a $50K-income prospect is poisoning our pixel data. We're paying to make the algorithm worse. After implementing: 2–4 week conditioning window (fewer calls), then call quality transforms. Close rates go from ~30% to 40%+. Show rates from ~50% to 70%+.

Implementation for CS

  • Add "$14,999/year" to VSL script — Ben states it directly
  • Add price to landing page above the fold
  • Add price to pre-call confirmation emails
  • Accept 2–4 week conditioning window — DO NOT panic and revert
  • Track call quality before and after (qualification rate, close rate per call)

Owner: Ben (VSL), Web team (landing page), Sanjay (monitoring during window)

#7 Hammer Them / Forester Content Ads

Urgent

What it is: Mass content distribution to manufacture warm audiences from cold traffic. Run content consumption ads (Video Views objective) alongside direct response. Warms cold audiences cheaply. Creates the "swarm effect" where prospects feel they already know Ben before the VSL.

Why CS needs this now

Without Hammer Them, cold traffic hits DR ads without knowing who Ben is. Show rates are low, close rates suffer, and cost per call is high. Jeremy's data: show rates go from ~50% to 60–78% when Hammer Them is running. At 117 calls/month, that's 12–33 more shows — translating to 4–10 additional Platinums/month.

Implementation for CS

  • Build content library: 20+ clips from Wealth Shift podcast, YouTube, Platinum days, US podcast appearances, testimonials
  • Create dedicated "Hammer Them" campaign in Meta — Video Views objective, NOT conversions
  • Allocate 30% of ad budget (~$21–24K/month) to content consumption
  • Build custom audiences from 25%+ video viewers — these become DR retargeting pools
  • Target cost per ThruPlay: $0.25–$0.50

Owner: Sanjay (campaign setup), Content team (clip production), Ben (source content)

#17 Meta Ad Restrictions Prep

Urgent

What it is: Prepare for Meta's financial services ad restrictions with backup domains, backup conversion events, compliance pre-checks, and third-party attribution.

Why CS needs this now

Digital asset education is firmly in Meta's "financial services" category. One account ban = revenue stops overnight. This isn't theoretical — it's when, not if. We need insurance before we scale spend to $70–82K/month.

Implementation for CS

  • Run ALL current ad copy through financial services compliance — remove return promises, guaranteed outcomes, urgency on financial products
  • Set up backup domain (e.g., bensimpson.com.au) with duplicate funnel
  • Configure backup conversion events in Meta Events Manager (PageView, ViewContent as fallbacks)
  • Set up Hyros or TripleWhale for third-party attribution
  • Build an appeal package: screenshots of all compliant ad content, landing pages, compliance records

Owner: Sanjay (compliance + backup events), Web team (backup domain), Tracking setup
