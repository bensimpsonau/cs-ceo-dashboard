Commission Model - Earn-Back Structure

How the Earn-Back Model Works

Reece and DT must earn their salary equivalent back in commissions before receiving any additional commission. First dollar of commission goes toward covering salary cost. Only after earning back the full salary amount do they earn 10% on everything above that threshold.

Example DT (~$13,000/mo) Reece (~$12,000/mo)
Monthly salary equivalent $13,000 $12,000
Sales needed to earn back salary (at 10%) $130,000 $120,000
Commission on first $130K / $120K $0 (covers salary) $0 (covers salary)
If they sell $200K total $7,000 (10% on $70K) $8,000 (10% on $80K)
If they sell $300K total $17,000 (10% on $170K) $18,000 (10% on $180K)
