1. Brand Identity

Mission

The world's most trusted digital asset education company. We help serious investors build generational wealth through systematic, research-driven frameworks. Free from hype, affiliates, and conflicting interests.

Core Values

  1. Independence above everything. Zero affiliates, zero token promotions. Our only revenue comes from members who stay.
  2. Skin in the game. We invest our own capital alongside members. Public portfolio. If we're wrong, we lose money too.
  3. Education over advice. Frameworks over signals. Conviction over dependency.
  4. Radical transparency. Audited returns. Honest when we're wrong.
  5. Long-term thinking. Generational wealth over decades, not days.
  6. Respect for intelligence. Our audience are successful professionals. We speak to them as peers.

2. Voice & Messaging

Language Rules

DON'T Say DO Say Why
Crypto Digital assets Professional positioning
Returns / gains Protection, preservation, growth ICP fears loss more than they chase gains
Get rich quick Build generational wealth Long-term horizon
Moon / lambo / WAGMI Long-term wealth building Signals retail culture
Financial advice Education / frameworks Legal and brand protection
Signals / tips Investment frameworks / research We teach systems, not trades
Em dashes (--) Dashes (-) or full stops Em dashes look like AI. Never use them.
Guru / expert Educator / peer investor We teach alongside, not above
Followers Members / community We build membership

Exception: "Bitcoin" is always "Bitcoin" (capital B). "Crypto" only acceptable quoting third parties or in legal contexts.

The 90/10 Rule

Free content = the WHAT and the WHY (builds trust).
Platinum = the HOW (delivers transformation).

Give away: Why digital assets matter, Bitcoin thesis, general allocation principles, exit strategy importance, common mistakes, self-custody fundamentals.

Reserve for Platinum: Specific allocation models, the Shift List, exact entry/exit triggers, portfolio alerts, 1:1 coaching, WhatsApp community, live events, personalised reviews.

Key Messaging

Message Use Case
"A professional framework for building generational wealth with digital assets" Core positioning
"We teach you how to think about digital assets. Not what to think." Education positioning
"6-year audited returns. Zero affiliates. 100% independent research." Trust anchor
"Join 402 high-net-worth investors who trust Collective Shift" Social proof
"Stop guessing. Start building a strategy that actually works." Pain-point lead

Ben's Voice

  • Conviction-driven: "I believe Bitcoin is the best savings technology ever created."
  • Peer investor: "I invest my own capital alongside every member."
  • Direct and honest: "Most of the market is noise. We filter out 98% of it."
  • Educator, not guru: "I'll teach you how to think, not what to think."
  • Empathetic: "I've made every mistake. So you don't have to."

The Ben Test (before publishing anything):
1. Would a successful 50-year-old business owner feel respected reading this?
2. Does this sound like Bloomberg or like a Telegram group?
3. Am I teaching a framework, or am I selling hype?
4. Would I be comfortable if a $5M net worth investor saw this?

ICP Language (from actual sales calls)

What They Say How We Use It

"I can't be with the techno bro" Emphasise professional positioning
"Building multi-generational wealth" Use "generational wealth" in headlines
"Too busy to research" Emphasise "we do the heavy lifting"

Template Types

All templates use: CS Dark (#0D0B14) background, Plus Jakarta Sans headings, Barlow body, Brand Orange accent, 60px safe zone padding, CS icon mark watermark at 15-20% opacity.

  1. Stat/Insight Post - Large hero number (PJS 800, 120px, white) with context line below. Orange accent bar at bottom.
  2. Carousel (10 slides) - Slide 1: bold hook + "SWIPE TO LEARN". Slides 2-8: one idea per slide on #1B1925 bg. Slide 9: numbered recap. Slide 10: CTA with CS logo and pill button.
  3. Quote/Testimonial Card - Orange left bar (4px). Large open quotation mark in orange. Quote in Barlow italic. Member info: first name + last initial only (circle photo, 48px).
  4. Educational Tip - Section label in orange. Title in white. Numbered list with orange circle bullets.
  5. Hero Slide - Split: bold headline left (60%), team photo right (40%) with orange glow behind. Pill CTA button.
  6. Team/Founder Profile - Headshot with orange glow. Name, role, 2-3 line bio.
  7. Lead-Gen / Quiz Promo - Bold problem headline + short copy + pill CTA. ManyChat keyword in orange text.
  8. Dark CTA / Offer Slide - Centered transformation headline + pill CTA + trust badges row.

4. YouTube Thumbnails

Thumbnails are a separate system from social templates. Optimise for CTR, not brand consistency.

Layout (70% of thumbnails)

  • Left 58%: 3-5 word headline (PJS 800, 100-120px, white + dark stroke)
  • Right 42%: Face cutout, shoulders up, eye line at 1/3 from top, orange glow behind

Text Rules

  • 3-5 words max. Minimum 90px for mobile readability.
  • White text with 2-4px dark stroke. Left-aligned in left 55-60% of frame.
  • Never overlap the face. Optional orange underline below key word.
  • Max 3 colours per thumbnail (background + text + 1 accent).

Expression Categories

Expression When to Use
Shock/surprise Unexpected data, contrarian takes
Curiosity Questions, "what if" content
Concern/serious Risk warnings, bear market
Excitement/smile Positive results, good news
Confidence/authority Educational, how-to, frameworks

Per-Presenter Style

  • Ben: Confidence, direct eye contact. Dark clothing. Orange glow. Bold declarative text. No logo.
  • Travis: Leadership energy, hand gestures. Teal accent. Framework-oriented text. Small CS icon.
  • Sarn: Educational, approachable. Lighter blue accents. Screen/interface imagery prominent. CS icon.
  • Matt: Analytical, thoughtful. Charts/data visible. Orange accent. CS icon.

A/B Testing

  1. Create 2 variants per video before upload
  2. Swap to variant B after 24 hours
  3. Compare CTR after 48 total hours (min 1,000 impressions each)
  4. Keep the higher-CTR thumbnail

5. Video Brand Standards

Intro (3-5 seconds max)

  • Cold Open (default for YouTube): Start with the hook (first 5-15 seconds pure content). Insert branded intro at 30-60 seconds. 3 seconds max.
  • Standard Intro: CS icon mark fades in > brief brand sound (1-2s) > presenter lower third. For LinkedIn, events, clips.

Outro (10-15 seconds)

  1. Bridge CTA on camera: "If you found this useful, here's what to do next..." (link to quiz, Platinum, subscribe, or next video)
  2. End screen zone with YouTube cards + subscribe button
  3. Brand close: CS logo + collectiveshift.io on dark background

Lower Thirds

  • 2px top accent line (#FC5C03)
  • Semi-transparent dark bar (rgba(13,11,20,0.85), 64px height)
  • Name: PJS 700, 20px, white. Title: Barlow 400, 14px, #B0B0B0
  • Position: bottom-left. Fade in at 0.5s, hold 5s, fade out.
  • Guests: add company/title on third line. Hold 7 seconds.

Captions/Subtitles

  • Font: Barlow Medium, 48-56px (long-form) or 56-64px (short-form)
  • White text with dark shadow. Center-bottom for long-form, center for short-form.
  • Keyword highlighting: 1-2 key words per block in orange (#FC5C03)

8. Content Approval Flow

Three Steps

Step 1: Self-Publish (No approval needed)
- Reposts of existing approved content
- Standard template posts with no new copy
- Factual market data updates (no directional opinion)
- Clips from published YouTube/podcast

Step 2: Team Lead Review (24-hour turnaround)
- New blog/article drafts, email drafts, carousels, video titles/descriptions
- Move Asana card to "Review" and tag reviewer
- Reviewer approves, requests changes, or escalates to Step 3

Step 3: Ben Approval Required
- Performance claims, return stats, member testimonials
- Pricing, promotions, limited-time campaigns
- Specific coins with directional opinion
- Press/media responses
- Anything that could be interpreted as financial advice

Emergency Publishing (market events): Matt or Ben can publish factual-only market updates without prior approval. Flag in Asana within 48 hours. No promotional CTAs. True emergencies only (20%+ drawdown, exchange collapse, critical security issue).

Brand Voice Checklist (before any Tier 2+ content)

  1. No em dashes anywhere
  2. "Digital assets" not "crypto"
  3. No hype language (moon, rocket, WAGMI, etc.)
  4. No income claims or return guarantees
  5. ICP-appropriate language
  6. 90/10 rule respected
  7. Correct brand colours and fonts
  8. Correct logo placement per sub-brand
  9. CTA from the approved list
  10. Testimonials properly anonymised (first name + last initial only)
