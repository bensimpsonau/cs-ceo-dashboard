PART 1: UTM Convention Guide

Standard Format

?utm_source=[platform]&utm_medium=[placement]&utm_campaign=[funnel]&utm_content=[specific]

Parameter Definitions

Parameter Purpose Format
utm_source Where the lead came from (platform) Lowercase, no spaces
utm_medium How/where on that platform Lowercase, underscores
utm_campaign Which funnel or campaign Lowercase, underscores
utm_content Specific variant or content piece Lowercase, underscores
