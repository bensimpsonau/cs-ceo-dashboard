Funnel Overview

The Problem We're Solving

Our ICP is a 40-59 year old business owner or senior professional with $500K-$2M+ net worth. They have significant wealth in traditional assets (property, stocks, super) but no coherent digital asset strategy. Their #1 pain is having no exit strategy (confidence: 2.8/10). They rate their own research ability at 3.7/10. They feel isolated making high-stakes decisions alone (support network: 3.2/10).

From sales calls: "Flying blind, need some help." "I can't be with the techno bro. And I can't be with the old world solid bankers."

Layer 1: Micro Magnets (Mapped to Blueprint Steps)

Design Principles (Taki Moore)

  • Complete solution to a TINY problem (not a big one)
  • Deliver a result in under 3 minutes
  • "Steal this and get this result now" format
  • NOT information-packed guides or PDFs

Micro Magnet 1: PROTECT Pillar - Maps to Blueprint Step 07 (Tax)

Title: "Steal my 3-step tax strategy that saved members $47K last financial year"

What it is: A 1-page decision tree (PDF or interactive). Three yes/no questions:
1. Should you be lending against your Bitcoin instead of selling? (No taxable event)
2. Should your digital assets be held personally or through a structure (trust, company, SMSF)?
3. The one tax rule most people break without knowing (every swap between assets is a CGT event)

Tiny problem it solves: "Am I structured properly, or am I about to get destroyed at tax time?"

Result in 3 minutes: Clear answer on whether their current holding structure is costing them money, plus one specific action.

Blueprint Step mapping: Step 07 (Tax: Staying Compliant). Uses the CGT basics, 12-month discount rule, and tracking tools (Koinly, CryptoTaxCalculator) from the actual Blueprint.

Blueprint exercise tie-in: The decision tree IS a simplified version of the Blueprint's Step 07 exercise: "Set up tax tracking."

ManyChat keyword: PROTECT (alternates: TAX, RISK)

Bridge to Layer 2: "Now you know how to protect what you have. But do you actually have a strategy for what to build? Take the 2-minute Digital Asset Readiness Assessment to find out where you stand across all 8 areas."

Compliance language (from Blueprint): "Educational purposes only. Digital assets carry significant risk. This does not constitute financial, tax, or legal advice. ABN 78 650 977 325. Not an AFS licensee."

Micro Magnet 2: BUILD Pillar - Maps to Blueprint Step 04 (How We Invest)

Title: "My Bitcoin allocation calculator - get your number in 60 seconds"

What it is: A simple calculator (interactive web page or GPT). Input: total investable assets, risk tolerance (conservative/moderate/aggressive), time horizon. Output: suggested allocation percentage for Bitcoin + digital assets.

Tiny problem it solves: "How much of my money should actually go into digital assets?"

Result in 3 minutes: A specific number - not "it depends."

Blueprint Step mapping: Step 04 (How We Invest: Risk Frameworks). Uses the 3 Investor Types and 4 Pre-Start Questions from the actual Blueprint.

Blueprint exercise tie-in: This IS the simplified version of Step 04's exercise: "Define your investor profile." The calculator maps their inputs to the Blueprint's investor type framework.

ManyChat keyword: BUILD (alternates: STRATEGY, ALLOCATE)

Bridge to Layer 2: "You've got your allocation number. But allocation without a framework is just gambling. The assessment scores you across all 8 areas of the Wealth Protection Blueprint - not just allocation, but exit strategy, tax, security, and more."

Micro Magnet 3: YIELD Pillar - Maps to Blueprint Step 06 (Send & Store) + Step 04 (Risk)

Title: "The 5-minute portfolio risk audit our Platinum members use quarterly"

What it is: A 1-page checklist (PDF). 7 yes/no questions covering:
1. Exchange risk - are your assets on a regulated exchange? (Blueprint Step 05 reference)
2. Custody - do you control your keys? (Blueprint Step 06: Self-custody + hardware wallets)
3. Concentration risk - is more than 40% in one asset? (Blueprint Step 04: 3 Investor Types)
4. Stablecoin exposure - which stablecoins and why it matters
5. Lending protocol risk
6. Insurance/backup access (Blueprint Step 06: security scaling)
7. Exit triggers - do you have written sell rules? (Blueprint Step 08: 3 sell strategy types)

Tiny problem it solves: "Is my portfolio actually safe, or am I one exchange collapse away from losing everything?"

Blueprint Step mapping: Primarily Step 06 (Send & Store) with elements of Step 04 (Risk Frameworks) and Step 08 (Sell Strategy).

ManyChat keyword: YIELD (alternates: INCOME, PASSIVE)

Bridge to Layer 2: "You've audited your risk. But risk management is just Step 6 of the 8-step Wealth Protection Blueprint. The assessment covers all 8 - including the one area where most people score lowest: exit strategy."

Micro Magnet 4: PROOF Pillar - Maps to Blueprint Assessment Data

Title: "See how 1,400+ professionals scored on the Digital Asset Readiness Assessment"

What it is: A 1-page infographic showing:
- Distribution of scores across all assessment takers
- Which archetype most people fall into (Hesitant First Allocator, typically)
- The #1 gap area: exit strategy (Blueprint Step 08) - consistently the lowest score
- What the top 10% (Structured Allocators) do differently: they've completed Steps 04, 07, and 08

Blueprint connection: The infographic references all 8 Blueprint steps and shows which steps each archetype typically has covered vs missing.

ManyChat keyword: PROOF (alternates: RESULTS, SCORE)

Bridge to Layer 2: "Want to see exactly where you fall? Take the same 2-minute assessment 1,400+ professionals have already completed. You'll get your score, your archetype, and the full 8-chapter Blueprint Guide."
