ManyChat Keywords on Social Posts

Every reel and social post should include a keyword CTA where it fits naturally. Rotate through these:

Keyword CTA Copy Use On
PROTECT "Comment PROTECT for the tax strategy decision tree" Tax, risk, security content
BUILD "Comment BUILD for the allocation calculator" Strategy, allocation, framework content
YIELD "Comment YIELD for the portfolio risk audit" Risk, security, exchange content
PROOF "Comment PROOF to see how 1,400+ investors scored" Results, social proof, performance content
BLUEPRINT "Comment BLUEPRINT for the full assessment" Any content (direct to assessment)

Rule: Not every post needs a keyword. Aim for 3-4 keyword CTAs per day across Ben's and Travis's reels. The rest can use "Link in bio" or no CTA.

Single-Link CTA Strategy

Bio link (all platforms): collectiveshift.co/cryptoblueprint (free training / Blueprint assessment)

Do not use Linktree or multi-link pages. One link. One destination. The assessment captures the email, segments by archetype, and delivers the Blueprint Guide. Everything else flows from there.

Exception: YouTube video descriptions can include multiple links (assessment, Platinum application, relevant resources).
