Social Media Header Strategy + Specs

Unified header design system for all 6 brands across all platforms

Design Philosophy

Every header communicates one thing: this is a team of real people who manage real money alongside their members. The group photo is the hero element across all channels. It creates visual unity. When someone finds Matt on Twitter, then visits the CS YouTube, then checks Ben's LinkedIn, the same team photo connects everything.

The aesthetic is wealth management, not trading floor. Think Bloomberg terminal meets private bank. Dark, clean, confident.

Core Design System

Colours

Element Colour Hex
Background CS Dark #0D0B14
Background gradient target CS Dark Alt #1B1925
Primary accent Brand Orange #FC5C03
Secondary accent / glow Brand Teal #0BF4B9
CTA button / link highlight Brand Blue #2404FC
Text primary White #FFFFFF
Text secondary Muted Light #B0B0B0

Typography

Element Font Weight Notes
Brand name / headline Plus Jakarta Sans 700-800 Large, left or right aligned
Tagline / descriptor Barlow 400-500 Below headline
CTA text Plus Jakarta Sans 600, uppercase Small, letter-spaced
Event/deck headlines Oswald or Bebas Neue 700 Only for presentation-style headers

Photography Direction

  • Group photo: Ben, Travis, Matt, Sarn standing together
  • Dark clothing, confident posture, natural expressions
  • Moody warm lighting. Subtle orange glow or gradient behind subjects
  • Shot from chest up or waist up depending on banner dimensions
  • No stock elements. No blockchain iconography. No candlestick charts.
  • Editorial, professional, team-oriented

Layout Principles

  • Group photo: left side or centre (60% of frame width)
  • Brand name / tagline: right side (40% of frame width)
  • CTA text: bottom bar or lower right
  • Teal accent: thin horizontal line (2-3px) separating CTA from main content, or subtle glow behind team
  • Orange accent: used sparingly (logo mark, underline on key word, or dot separator)
  • Safe zone padding: 60px minimum from all edges (platform-specific safe areas noted below)

DESIGN PRODUCTION CHECKLIST

What the designer needs:

  1. Group photo shoot of Ben, Travis Thomas, Matt Willemsen, Sarn Elliott, and Aaron Berghuber (Bergs) (PRIORITY)
    - Dark clothing (black or navy)
    - Studio setting with controllable lighting
    - Multiple poses: standing together, seated around table, walking
    - Shot on dark/neutral background (easier to composite onto headers)
    - Both landscape and portrait crops
    - Minimum resolution: 4000px on longest edge

  2. Ben + Bergs photo for The Wealth Shift headers
    - Same aesthetic as team photo
    - Conversational pose (not stiff portrait)

  3. Brand assets
    - cs-logo-white.png (vector/SVG preferred)
    - cs_icon_mark.png
    - Plus Jakarta Sans and Barlow font files

  4. Template files (Figma or Photoshop)
    - Master template with all dimensions pre-set
    - Smart object for team photo (swap easily)
    - Colour swatches loaded
    - Grid guides for safe areas

Production Priority Order:

  1. YouTube banners (Ben, CS, Wealth Shift) - highest visibility
  2. Twitter/X headers (Ben, CS) - most frequently viewed
  3. LinkedIn banners (Ben, CS) - highest-value audience
  4. Facebook cover (CS) - supports paid ads
  5. Instagram highlight covers - quick wins, immediate implementation
