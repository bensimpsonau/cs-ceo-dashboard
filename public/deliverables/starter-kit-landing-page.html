SECTION 8: FAQ

Q: Is this financial advice?

No. This is education. We teach you how to think about digital assets, not what to do with your money. We'll show you frameworks and give you tools to make your own informed decisions. Educational purposes only. ABN 78 650 977 325. Not an AFS licensee.

Q: Do I need any prior experience with digital assets?

Zero. The Starter Kit starts from absolute basics and builds up. If you know nothing about Bitcoin or digital assets, you're the exact person this was built for.

Q: How much money do I need to get started?

You can buy as little as $50 worth of Bitcoin. Bitcoin is divisible to 8 decimal places (1 BTC = 100,000,000 sats). You don't need thousands of dollars to start. The investment plan you build on Day 4 will be based on your personal situation.

Q: Which exchanges do you cover?

We focus on three global exchanges: Coinbase (most trusted, beginner-friendly), Kraken (strong security, competitive fees), and Binance (lowest fees, most options). The course helps you choose the one that's right for your country and situation.

Q: What if I already own some Bitcoin?

If you bought without a framework, the Starter Kit will help you understand what you have and build a proper plan around it. But if you already have a meaningful portfolio ($50K+), the Accelerator or Platinum membership might be a better fit. Take the free Digital Asset Readiness Assessment to find out.

Q: Can I go at my own pace?

Yes. The course is designed for 7 days, but you have lifetime access. If you need to take Day 3 and Day 4 over a weekend instead, that's fine. The daily structure is there to keep momentum, not to pressure you.

Q: What happens after Day 7?

On Day 7, you'll see the complete 8-step Shift OS framework and exactly where you stand. You'll have completed 4 of the 8 steps. For the remaining 4 (altcoin evaluation, full risk frameworks, tax compliance, and sell strategy), you can take the free assessment, book a strategy call, or continue learning with our free content. There's no automatic upsell or recurring charge.
