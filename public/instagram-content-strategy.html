DM Keyword Strategy

Each CTA keyword triggers an automated DM sequence via ManyChat or similar. The keyword should match the content topic to qualify intent. Rotate keywords across content to segment the audience.

"INVEST" General interest. Entry point to the funnel. Sends overview of Collective Shift.
"STRATEGY" Wants a framework. Send allocation strategy teaser + booking link.
"ALLOCATE" Portfolio-focused. Send the allocation framework PDF + consult CTA.
"THESIS" Research-driven. Send the Bitcoin investment thesis one-pager.
"BLUEPRINT" Wants the full plan. Send the HNW Blueprint PDF + membership CTA.
"QUIZ" Self-assessment. Send a 5-question quiz link that segments into investor type.
DM Automation Flow

Step 1: User DMs keyword from CTA in Reel/Carousel.

Step 2: Auto-reply with a value-first resource (PDF, quiz link, or short video).

Step 3: Follow-up message 24 hours later: "Did you get a chance to check it out? Happy to answer any questions."

Step 4: If engaged, present Collective Shift membership or consultation booking.

Step 5: Tag in CRM (Pipedrive) for sales follow-up on high-intent leads.
