Hook → Body → CTA Formula (Every Piece of Content)
1. Hook (0-3 seconds)
Bold, specific claim that stops the scroll. Use data, not opinion. Fear + agency formula: [Scary Data] + [Here's What Smart Money Is Doing]
Example: "The stock market is at all time highs. And that's the problem."
2. Body (3-80 seconds)
6-step argument: Hook → Real data → Root cause → Objection flip → Pivot to solution → Proof. End on an open loop (unresolved question).
Example: "M2 supply at $22.7T... dollar lost 52% vs gold... 145 companies holding Bitcoin..."
3. CTA (Final 10 seconds)
Drive to the Wealth Shift Assessment. Soft, value-first CTA. Never "buy now." Always "I've put together a free assessment."
Example: "I've put together a free wealth assessment that shows you exactly where your portfolio stands. Link in bio."

07 / The Micromagnet Lead System

Why Traditional Lead Magnets Are Dead (And What We're Doing Instead)

Traditional lead magnets — ultimate guides, 5-day challenges, free strategy sessions — are dead. Nobody finishes a 42-page PDF. Nobody has time for a challenge. Everyone knows a "free strategy session" is a sales call. We're replacing all of it with Micromagnets: tiny tools that solve one specific problem in under 2 minutes, require zero learning, and deliver an instant result. The test is simple — it's a light switch, not an electrician's course.

The 4 Types of Lead Magnet — Only 1 Works Now

Based on Taki Moore's Micromagnet framework. Our ICP is time-poor, high-net-worth, and sceptical. They won't sit through a course or fall for a disguised sales call.

❌ Trap
Free Strategy Session
Everyone knows it's a sales call. Our ICP avoids these on instinct. Zero trust built.
❌ Crap
Newsletter Opt-In
Safe but worthless. "Join our newsletter" is table stakes, not a value proposition.
❌ Gap
Free Course / 5-Day Challenge
Valuable but too much effort. Our ICP doesn't have 5 days. Completion rate: <10%. "Ain't nobody got time for that."
✅ Micromagnet
Instant Tool — Results in a Snap
Safe + instant + solves one tiny problem + no learning required. Feels like stealing a tool, not taking a course. Consumed in under 2 minutes. This is what we're building.
What Makes a Great Micromagnet
• Solves a SPLINTER (tiny specific pain point), not a broad problem
• Consumed in 120 seconds or less
• No information — just OUTCOME
• Feels like a TOOL not a course ("steal this" not "learn this")
• Zero friction: no sales call, no signup forms, just get the result
• Title formula: "Steal/Copy/Get [specific thing] in [timeframe] without [pain]"
🚫 What We're NOT Building
Free WordPress beginners course, free multi-day challenges, 42-page ultimate guides — these are all Gap magnets in Taki's framework. They're information-packed, require effort, and nobody finishes them. Our ICP doesn't have 5 days. They need a result in 3 minutes. Don't build courses as lead magnets. Build tools.
