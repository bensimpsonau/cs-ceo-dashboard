Hook → Body → CTA Formula (Every Piece of Content)
1. Hook (0-3 seconds)
Bold, specific claim that stops the scroll. Use data, not opinion. Fear + agency formula: [Scary Data] + [Here's What Smart Money Is Doing]
Example: "The stock market is at all time highs. And that's the problem."
2. Body (3-80 seconds)
6-step argument: Hook → Real data → Root cause → Objection flip → Pivot to solution → Proof. End on an open loop (unresolved question).
Example: "M2 supply at $22.7T... dollar lost 52% vs gold... 145 companies holding Bitcoin..."
3. CTA (Final 10 seconds)
Drive to the Wealth Shift Assessment. Soft, value-first CTA. Never "buy now." Always "I've put together a free assessment."
Example: "I've put together a free wealth assessment that shows you exactly where your portfolio stands. Link in bio."

The 8 Principles We Are Adopting
  1. Named proprietary framework. "The Wealth Shift Blueprint" is now the branded lead magnet name. Consistently named everywhere.
  2. YouTube as primary funnel engine. Long-form builds trust faster than any other format. Ben is the anchor.
  3. Fear + agency hook formula. [Scary Data] + [Here's What I'm Doing]. Not fear alone. Always pair with agency and a framework.
  4. What/Why free, How paid. 90/10 principle. Give 10% of the system for free (massive value). Platinum delivers the 90% (the execution).
  1. Confirmation page as conversion tool. Not a dead end. Active page with Ben video, social proof, and next-step CTA.
  2. Multi-domain authority stack. YouTube + podcast + media + awards + no-affiliate stance = compounding trust.
  3. Multiple entry points per bucket. Different headlines for different ICP sub-segments. One funnel, many doors.
  4. Branded quiz as qualifier. "The Wealth Shift Assessment" not "quiz." Professional framing for HNW audience.
Key Differentiation: Why We Are Not Mark Moss
His tone is anti-establishment, freedom-maximalist, and US-centric. Our ICP is an Australian professional who values pragmatism over politics. We share the same funnel mechanics but our positioning is completely different: calm authority, data-driven education, zero affiliates, and a focus on wealth goals (not freedom ideology). Our ICP does not want a revolution. They want a framework they can trust.
