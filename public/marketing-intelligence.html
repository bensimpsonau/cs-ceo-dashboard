ICP & Creative Intelligence

🎯 ICP: "The Legacy Builder"

  • Age 45–65, predominantly male, HNW
  • $100k–$500k+ portfolio in traditional assets (stocks, property)
  • $250k+ annual income, 7-figure+ net worth
  • 5+ year time horizon — strategic, not speculative
  • Goals: generational wealth, inflation hedge, financial sovereignty
  • Fears: missing the crypto opportunity, volatility, making a mistake, information overload
  • Desires: trusted guide, structured system, community of peers
  • Key message: "You've succeeded in stocks & property — here's the next logical step"
  • Geography: Australia dominant, growing UK/NZ/US/CA pipeline
  • Core tension: FOMO vs fear of making a costly mistake

🎨 Winning Creative Angles

  • #1 Silent Thief (inflation angle) — Vid3.1-SilentThief_ClientCopy: 3,266x ROAS. "Your wealth is being stolen by inflation." Dramatic storytelling + client copy.
  • #2 Building Legacy (generational wealth) — Vid3.3-BuildingLegacy: top revenue & profit. "Build a digital legacy for your family."
  • #3 Ben Simpson Authority — Video ads featuring Ben build deep trust. Years of content create a moat no competitor can replicate.
  • #4 Platinum Member Testimonials — Video testimonials from members matching ICP. "I was a successful investor but completely lost in crypto."
  • Underperformers: NIU series & VidPod series — low ROAS, messaging didn't resonate.
  • Key insight: Client-based copy outperforms generic. AUNZ targeting outperforms USCA for cold audiences.
  • #1 hook theme: Exit strategy / "when to sell" — strongest desire across all prospects.
