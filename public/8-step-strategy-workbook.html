Where does this fit in the education system?

The Collective Shift education system is built in three phases: Learn (understand digital assets and markets), Research (evaluate specific assets), and Strategy (build and execute your personal plan).

This workbook is Phase 3 — Strategy. It's where everything comes together. You've learned the frameworks, you understand your options — now you commit your approach to paper so you have something to return to when markets test you.
