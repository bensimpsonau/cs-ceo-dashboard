Section 03

Scoring System

Qualifier Questions (Q1-Q3)

Unscored. Used for personalisation only: experience level, primary goal, and capital outlook feed into the results intro paragraph and CRM tags.

Scored Questions (Q4-Q9)

1-4 points each. Maximum total: 24. Score determines the archetype and personalised messaging on the results page.

Cautious Observer 6 - 10

Strong on caution. Gap: no structured entry framework.

"You're asking the right questions. Here's the framework to act on them."

Hesitant First Allocator 11 - 15

Ready to move. Gap: missing conviction and process.

"The intent is there. The structure isn't yet."

Reactive Dabbler 16 - 20

Has experience. Gap: reactive decisions, no system.

"You've started. Now it's time to build a strategy around what you have."

Structured Allocator 21 - 24

Strong foundation. Gap: optimising and protecting gains.

"The framework is working. The next level is refinement and risk protection."
