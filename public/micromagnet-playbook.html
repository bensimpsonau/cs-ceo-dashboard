01 / Framework
What Is a Micromagnet?

A micromagnet is a complete solution to a TINY problem, consumed in under 3 minutes, that requires zero learning and delivers an instant result. The test: it's a light switch — they don't need to understand electricity, they just need the lights to come on.

The 4 Types of Lead Magnet — Only 1 Works Now

Source: Taki Moore's Micromagnet framework. Our ICP is 45-65, HNW, time-poor, and deeply sceptical of internet marketing tactics.

TypeExampleProblemVerdict
🪤 Trap Free strategy session Everyone knows it's a sales call. Our ICP avoids these on instinct. DEAD
💩 Crap Newsletter opt-in Safe but valueless. "Join our newsletter" is table stakes, not a value prop. DEAD
📚 Gap Free course / 5-day challenge / 42-page PDF Valuable but too much effort. Completion rate <10%. Our ICP doesn't have 5 days. DEAD
🧲 Micromagnet Calculator, scorecard, custom GPT, 1-page cheat sheet Safe + instant + solves one tiny problem + no learning required ✅ THIS
What Makes a Great Micromagnet
• Solves a SPLINTER (tiny specific pain point), not a broad problem
• Consumed in 120 seconds or less (our GPT: 3 minutes max)
• No information — just OUTCOME
• Feels like a TOOL not a course ("steal this" not "learn this")
• Zero friction: no sales call, no lengthy signup forms
• Title formula: "Steal/Copy/Get [specific thing] in [timeframe] without [pain]"

02 / Anti-Patterns
What We're NOT Building
🚫 Do Not Build These as Lead Magnets

Free WordPress beginners course — This was flagged as a content idea but it's a classic Gap magnet. Information-packed, long, nobody finishes it. Our ICP doesn't want to learn WordPress. They want a result.

5-day email challenges — Completion rate <10%. Day 1 gets 60% opens. Day 5 gets 8%. Dead format for time-poor HNW audience.

"Ultimate Guide to Digital Assets" (42-page PDF) — Nobody reads these. They download them, feel good for 3 seconds, then forget they exist. Zero trust built.

Free strategy sessions disguised as "discovery calls" — Our ICP sees through this immediately. It's a Trap magnet. Don't do it at the top of funnel.

Rule: If it requires more than 3 minutes of their time before they get a result, it's not a micromagnet. Don't build it as one.

08 / The Post Formula
✋ The Hand Raiser Formula

Based on Leo Burnett's 60-year-old advertising formula. Taki Moore calls the GPT that writes these "The Hand Raiser Automatic." This is the structure for every post that promotes a micromagnet — text, carousel, Reel, ad, YouTube description. Same formula, every time.

1. Here's what I got. [Name the tool/asset]
2. Here's what it'll do for you. [Specific outcome in a timeframe]
3. Here's how to get it. [Comment WORD / click link / DM me]
Rules for Using the Formula
• Speak directly to the SITUATION and FRUSTRATION of the ICP
• Don't teach. Don't explain. Just show them the result they'll get.
• The CTA is always: Comment [KEYWORD] → ManyChat DMs the link
• Works across: text posts, carousels, Reels, ads, YouTube descriptions, X threads
• Every micromagnet promotion follows this structure. No exceptions.
