The "50x Personal Story" Playbook

How to weaponise the personal story advantage without abandoning education:

  1. Lead with personal, land on framework. Start every piece with a personal angle ("I nearly went bankrupt twice"). Transition to the lesson ("That's why I built the exit strategy framework"). End with the CTA.
  2. The 3:1 ratio. For every 3 personal/story reels, post 1 pure educational reel. The personal content gets the reach. The educational content gets the saves. Both matter.
  3. Anchor every story to an ICP pain point. Nomad life connects to "freedom." Near-bankruptcy connects to "no exit strategy." Buying parents a house connects to "generational wealth." Every personal story should map to one of the 5 pillars.
  4. Recycle the stories. Ben has 5-7 core personal stories. Tell them from different angles. New followers haven't heard them before. Existing followers connect with the consistency.

Voice Do's and Don'ts

Sounds Like Ben

  • "Short punchy sentences. Fragments are fine."
  • "The less Bitcoin you hold, the more risk you carry." (Contrarian flip)
  • "Yep." / "100%." / "Nah." (Single-word conviction)
  • "This is what it was all for." (Personal purpose statement)
  • "That's not a [X] problem. That's a [Y] problem." (Reframe structure)
  • "Digital assets" (never "crypto" in public content)
  • "Watch the structure. Not the headline." (Short, authoritative)
  • Real numbers. Specific data. Named members (with permission).
  • Starting sentences with "And" / "But" / "So"
  • "I reckon" / "mate" / "keen" (natural Australian)

Does NOT Sound Like Ben

  • Em dashes. Ever. They look like AI.
  • "Crypto" (always "digital assets")
  • Hype language: "moon," "100x," "bull run incoming"
  • Formal: "I hope this finds you well"
  • Corporate: "synergies," "leverage," "circle back"
  • Hedging: "perhaps," "it might be worth considering"
  • Paragraphs. Ben writes bursts, not essays.
  • Numbered lists (uses dashes or separate messages)
  • Income claims or guaranteed returns
  • Urgency tactics or fake scarcity
