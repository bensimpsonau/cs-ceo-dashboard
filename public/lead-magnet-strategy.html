01 / Strategy Overview

The Full Funnel: Cold to Platinum

Organic and paid are not separate strategies. They feed the same funnel. Organic proves which hooks and magnets convert. Paid amplifies the winners. Both channels deliver leads into the same email nurture and setter pipeline. The only difference is the entry point.

Funnel Architecture
Step 1: Cold Audience
HNW Australians, 35-60, $500K+ investable. Self-directed investors who know digital assets matter but lack professional infrastructure. Reached via Hammer Them content ads (paid) or organic Reels/YouTube (organic).
↓
Step 2: TOF Ad or Organic Reel
Organic: Ben publishes IG Reel with Hand Raiser CTA. "Comment [KEYWORD] and I will DM you the link." Paid: Meta lead gen ad drives to landing page with email capture and lead magnet download.
↓
Step 3: Lead Magnet Delivery
Organic: ManyChat DM delivers link within 60 seconds. Paid: Landing page instant download + email confirmation. Both qualify: tools ask about portfolio size, goals, risk. The lead magnet diagnoses. It does not prescribe.
↓
Step 4: 14-Day Email Nurture
Customer.io sequence: 9 emails over 14 days. Delivers value, builds trust, handles objections. Every email reinforces the gap between what the lead magnet showed and what Platinum solves. Soft CTA on Day 13. Hard CTA on Day 14.
↓
Step 5: Setter Call
15-20 min qualification call. Setter has full context: which lead magnet, what result, email engagement signals. Confirms fit, budget awareness, intent. If qualified, books closer call.
↓
Step 6: Closer → Platinum $14,999
30-45 min strategy session. Deep portfolio review. Personalised recommendations. The close. Target: 25-35% close rate. Revenue per close: $14,999.
Key principle: Lead magnets are qualification tools, not just list builders. Every question the tool asks, every metric it surfaces, and every word in the copy is designed to attract people who can afford $14,999 and repel people who cannot. A lead magnet that generates 500 unqualified leads is worse than one that generates 50 HNW investors who match the ICP. Engineering the offer means embedding qualification into the hook, the copy, and the tool itself.

02 / The 5 Lead Magnets

Each Magnet Qualifies for the Same Destination

Five different entry points, one funnel. Each lead magnet targets a different pain bucket but qualifies for the same outcome: a setter call that leads to Platinum. The keyword triggers ManyChat (organic) or the landing page captures email (paid). Both paths converge at the email nurture sequence.

🤖
Keyword: STRATEGY  Hero Magnet
Digital Asset Portfolio Strategy Guide
Target Audience Segment

Broadest appeal. HNW investors who know digital assets matter but have never built a systematic strategy. The "where do I start?" crowd. Most common keyword. Highest volume potential.

Value Proposition

"I built a tool that creates your personalised digital asset strategy in 3 minutes." Asks 5 questions about portfolio, goals, risk. Generates a personalised strategy using the same 8-step framework 3,000+ Platinum members use. Genuinely useful but raises more questions than it answers.

Delivery Method

Organic: ManyChat DM with Custom GPT link. Paid: Landing page with email gate, then redirect to GPT tool.

What It Qualifies For

The strategy diagnoses but cannot implement. "Your strategy suggests 12% allocation with a conservative BTC/ETH approach. But implementing this correctly requires tax structure decisions, entry timing, and rebalancing frameworks that go beyond what any tool can provide." That gap drives the setter call.

🛡
Keyword: SCORECARD  Self-Assessment
Digital Asset Readiness Scorecard
Target Audience Segment

HNW investors who think their portfolio is protected. The "am I actually exposed?" crowd. Uses "HNW investors" in the hook directly, which pre-qualifies. A 21-year-old with $500 scrolls past. A 45-year-old surgeon with $3M in property stops.

Value Proposition

"Most HNW investors think their portfolio is protected from inflation. Most are wrong." 6 questions about current allocation. Instant score (1-10). Average score: 4.2/10. Shows exactly where the portfolio is exposed.

Delivery Method

Organic: ManyChat DM with quiz/assessment link. Paid: Landing page with email gate, then redirect to scorecard.

What It Qualifies For

A low score creates urgency. "You scored 3.8 out of 10. Here is why." The scorecard flags the problem but cannot fix it. The setter call maps out what to do about it.

🧮
Keyword: CALCULATOR  High Intent
Bitcoin SMSF Allocation Calculator
Target Audience Segment

SMSF holders and HNW investors with a real allocation question. Hardest pre-qualifier of all five: the calculator requires total portfolio value input. Someone with $200 has no reason to use an allocation calculator. Someone with $1M+ has a genuine question about what percentage should be in digital assets. Highest viral potential because people screenshot and share results.

Value Proposition

"How much of my portfolio should be in digital assets? We built a calculator that answers it in 60 seconds." Input total portfolio + age + risk profile + current mix. Output: recommended allocation %, BTC/ETH/alt split, and comparison to institutional-grade portfolios.

Organic: ManyChat DM with calculator link. Paid: Landing page with email gate, then redirect to HTML calculator tool.

What It Qualifies For

The calculator gives the what. "8-12% in digital assets." The next question is always: "How do I actually get there? Timing, tax structure, which platforms, rebalancing." That is the setter call.

04 / Split Test Ideas

For each of the 5 lead magnets: one ManyChat DM hook test, one paid ad angle test, and one landing page headline test. All copy is genuinely different, not reworded. Run each test with a minimum of 200 impressions per variant before calling a winner.

05 / Paid TOF Ad Strategy

Jeremy's Framework Applied to CS Lead Magnets

Ad objective: lead generation, not awareness. Organic proves which hooks convert. Paid amplifies the winners. Never put paid behind a lead magnet that has not been tested organically first. The IG comment data tells you what the market wants. The paid budget tells the market to give you more of it.

4 Ad Types to Run
Type 1 Video View Retargeting Ads
ObjectiveVideo Views / ThruPlay. Build warm audiences from cold traffic at scale. This is the "Hammer Them" layer.
AudienceCold: broad interests (personal finance, property investment, ASX, SMSF). Then retarget 25%+ viewers into lead magnet ads.
Budget30% of total TOF ad spend. Target cost per ThruPlay: $0.25-$0.50.
Hook/AngleBen's podcast clips, market commentary, Platinum member testimonials, educational clips on allocation and tax. Content only. No CTA. No pitch.
CTANone. The goal is consumption, not clicks. These viewers become the warm audience for Type 2.
Type 2 Lead Magnet Direct Ads
ObjectiveLead generation. Drive to lead magnet landing page with email capture. "Free: [Lead Magnet Name]".
AudienceWarm: 25%+ video viewers from Type 1, IG/FB engagers (30-day), email subscriber lookalike. Do NOT run to cold.
Budget40% of total TOF ad spend. Target CPL: under $30.
Hook/AngleLead with the specific tool. "Get your personalised digital asset strategy in 3 minutes." "Find out your inflation protection score." Use the post variations from Section 2 as creative templates.
CTA"Get Your Free [Strategy/Scorecard/Allocation/Blueprint/Tax Guide]" — one button, one page, one action.
Type 3 Venus Fly Trap Sequence
ObjectiveWarm cold audiences through a 3-video content sequence before pitching. V1 Educate, V2 Prove, V3 Trust, then DR (book a call).
AudienceCold: broad HNW interests. Sequence narrows: V1 viewers (25%+) see V2, V2 viewers see V3, V3 viewers see DR. 75-90% drop-off per step is normal.
Budget20% of total TOF ad spend. V1 gets the largest share (largest audience, cheapest views).
Hook/AngleV1 (Educate): "Most HNW investors are missing a critical piece of their portfolio infrastructure. Here is what the top 1% are doing with digital assets."
