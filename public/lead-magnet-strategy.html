01 / Hand Raiser Framework

Engineer the Offer So Only High-Intent People Opt In

Jeremy Haynes' Hand Raiser framework is the foundation. Every lead magnet must pre-qualify. The goal is not maximum volume. The goal is maximum quality. A lead magnet that generates 500 low-intent leads is worse than one that generates 50 high-intent leads who match the ICP. Engineering the offer means embedding qualification into the hook, the copy, and the tool itself.

Full Funnel Architecture: Lead magnets are the pre-VSL qualification layer. The complete path is: (1) Hammer Them ads warm up cold audiences with content consumption, (2) Lead Magnets (STRATEGY/SCORECARD/CALCULATOR/BLUEPRINT/TAX) qualify via ManyChat DM, (3) Qualified leads drive to the VSL where $14,999 is stated and the CTA is to book a call, (4) Leads who are not ready enter the nurture sequence and come back to the VSL later, (5) Setter qualifies, Closer converts to Platinum $14,999. The newsletter acts as the re-engagement layer for leads who hit the VSL but did not book. Every touchpoint in this system exists to move the right people toward the VSL with higher intent.
Hand Raiser Formula (Every Post, Every Ad, Every Time)

This is the structure that makes the lead magnet system work. It filters for intent. People who comment the keyword are self-selecting as interested. People who complete the tool are self-selecting as engaged. People who respond to the follow-up are self-selecting as ready. Each step narrows the funnel to only the right people.

ElementRuleWhy
1. Here is what I got Name the tool. Be specific. "I built a tool that creates your personalised digital asset strategy in 3 minutes." Specificity signals quality. Vague offers attract vague people.
2. Here is what it does for you State the specific outcome in a specific timeframe. "6 questions. Instant score. Shows you the gaps." Not "learn about digital assets." Outcome language attracts action-takers. Process language attracts browsers.
3. Here is who it is for Embed the ICP into the copy. "The same framework our 3,000+ members use to structure $500K-$5M+ portfolios." This is not for broke teenagers. The language pre-qualifies. People who do not match the ICP self-select out. Saves setter time and protects pixel quality.
4. Here is how to get it "Comment [KEYWORD] and I will DM you the link." One action. No forms. No landing pages. No email required. Lowest possible friction for the right people. Highest possible signal (a public comment is a commitment).
Pre-qualification embedded in every magnet: Each tool is designed for HNW investors who are serious about building digital asset infrastructure. The language says it. The tool experience reinforces it. The Strategy Tool asks about portfolio size and risk tolerance. The Scorecard assesses existing wealth exposure. The Calculator asks for total portfolio value. The Blueprint references $500K-$5M+ portfolios. The Tax playbook covers SMSF, trust, and individual structures that only apply to people with real money. If someone without $500K uses the tool, they self-select out at the knowledge gap stage. They do not book a call. They do not waste a setter's time. The system qualifies automatically.

Nobody goes from cold to $14,999. They go from cold to free tool to knowledge gap to VSL to setter call to Platinum. Each step is an easy yes.

The Costco Model: Costco gives you free samples. You taste the sample. You buy the product. The sample is free but it is NOT the product. The lead magnet is the sample. It must be genuinely good (so they trust you) but incomplete (so they want more). If the sample is the entire meal, nobody buys. If the sample is stale, nobody trusts you. The tool diagnoses. Platinum prescribes and implements. Get this balance right and the entire funnel works.
