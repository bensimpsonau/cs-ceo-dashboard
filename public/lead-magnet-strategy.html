The Lead Magnet Concept

"Your Digital Asset Wealth Blueprint" - A personalized quiz that segments the lead, delivers a tailored output, and funnels them into a 1-hour masterclass. This is the core offer.

The 3-Step Flow

Step 1: The Quiz

5-7 interactive questions that segment the lead by experience, goals, net worth, and risk appetite. Takes under 2 minutes. Feels personalized, not generic.

INTERACTIVE SEGMENTATION
Step 2: Your Blueprint

Auto-generated based on quiz answers. Includes investor profile type, recommended allocation framework, top 3 priorities, and a clear next-step CTA. Emailed as a PDF.

PERSONALIZED PDF DELIVERY
Step 3: The Masterclass

Thank you page plays a pre-recorded 1-hour training from Ben. Covers the Shift OS foundations, the 3 mistakes HNW investors make, and a case study. Ends with CTA: book a strategy session.

1-HOUR VIDEO HIGH TRUST
Why this works for our ICP: High-net-worth investors (35-55, $1M+ net worth) want structure and personalization, not generic content. The quiz makes them feel understood. The blueprint gives immediate value. The masterclass builds enough trust to book a call. This mirrors the "Deal Analysis Toolkit" approach but tailored for digital assets education.

What the Quiz Captures

Lead Data:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Investment experience level
  • Net worth range
  • Allocation intent (next 12 months)

Segmentation Data:

  • Primary goal (preservation / growth / diversification / legacy)
  • Biggest fear (mistakes / FOMO / overload / no exit plan)
  • Current portfolio mix (property / stocks / super / business)
  • Success vision (freedom / legacy / diversification / inflation)

Masterclass Content Outline

1-hour pre-recorded training. Plays on the thank-you page after quiz completion. This is where the real trust is built.

0-5 min
Ben's Story (Relatable, Authentic)
How Ben went from traditional finance to building Collective Shift. Personal, vulnerable, relatable. Establishes credibility through experience, not credentials.
5-15 min
Why Digital Assets Matter NOW
Macro thesis: inflation, currency debasement, institutional adoption. Data-driven, not hype. Show the Bloomberg Terminal, the ETF inflows, the central bank moves. Make the case that ignoring digital assets is the risky move.
15-25 min
The 3 Mistakes HNW Investors Make
1) Buying at the top (chasing green candles). 2) No exit strategy (holding forever or panic selling). 3) Following hype (meme coins, influencer picks, "100x" promises). Real examples. Real losses. Makes the audience feel seen.
25-40 min
The Shift OS Framework (7-Step System Overview)
Give the WHAT, hold the HOW. Overview of the 7 steps: Research, Allocate, Buy, Store, Monitor, Exit, Review. Show the framework visually. Explain what each step does and why it matters. Do NOT give the detailed execution. This is the "taste" that makes them want the full program.
40-50 min
Case Study Walkthrough
How a $2M net worth property investor built a $500K digital asset portfolio using the Shift OS. Timeline, entry points, allocation decisions, exit strategy. Real numbers (anonymized). Shows what is possible with the right system.
50-55 min
Q&A: Top Objections
Pre-recorded answers to the top 5 objections: "Is it too late?" / "Is it too risky?" / "What about regulation?" / "I do not have time to manage it" / "My advisor says not to." Address each head-on with data and member stories.
55-60 min
CTA: Book Your Strategy Session
Soft, authentic close. "If this resonated with you, the next step is a free strategy session with our team. No pressure, no sales pitch. We will review your blueprint, answer your questions, and see if Platinum is the right fit." Show the booking link. Reiterate the value.
Production notes: Film in a professional setting (not a bedroom). Use screen shares for data slides. Keep Ben in frame at least 70% of the time. Natural, conversational tone. No teleprompter feel. Consider adding chapter markers for skip-ahead.
