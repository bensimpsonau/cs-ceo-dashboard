Why Most Investors Get Altcoins Badly Wrong

TRAP 1

Wrong Information Sources

Most crypto content is paid promotion. Influencers earn affiliate income. Telegram "alpha" groups are often exit liquidity. If you don't know who's paying, assume someone is.

No Evaluation Framework

A token that's "pumping" is not a thesis. Real evaluation: What problem? Who's the team? Tokenomics? Competitors? Realistic market size?

You buy a token that 10x's. But 15% annual inflation quietly destroys your gains. Understanding emission schedules is non-negotiable.

Three Things to Do Before Q2 2026 Is Over

01

Define Your Bitcoin Allocation

  • Decide what % of your portfolio belongs in BTC
  • Use institutional range: 1-5% conservative, up to 10% conviction
  • Factor in timeline, risk tolerance, existing portfolio
  • Write it down — not "some," an actual number
02

Audit Your Altcoin Positions

  • For every altcoin: What's my target? Timeframe? What changes my mind?
  • If you can't answer all three, you have hope — not a thesis
  • Cut positions you can't defend
  • Check tokenomics — is inflation eating your gains?
03

Build Your Sell Strategy NOW

  • A sell strategy built in a bull market is worth 10x one built in panic
  • Set price-based triggers for each position
  • Set condition-based triggers (what would make you exit?)
  • Write it down. Keep it where you'll actually look at it.

EXIT STRATEGY

The Biggest Mistake: Never Building an Exit

2.4 / 5
AVERAGE SELL STRATEGY CONFIDENCE
PRICE-BASED

Scale Out Selling

Sell in portions as price rises (e.g. 25% at 2x, 25% at 3x). Set stop-loss in advance. Don't move goalposts.

CONDITION-BASED

Fundamental Changes

Project fundamentals change. Overvaluation signals. Liquidity tightening = rotate to BTC or stablecoins.

TIME-BASED

Cycle & Milestones

Sell into strength before historically weak periods. Personal milestones are valid triggers regardless of market.

Scale out gradually. Nobody sells at the absolute top. Selling into strength is strategy, not a mistake.
