→ Managed by: position sizing, staged entry, time horizon

Security Risk

Exchange failure, theft, seed phrase loss

→ Managed by: hardware wallet, seed phrase, exchange hygiene

Liquidity Risk

Can't exit when you need to

→ Managed by: BTC/ETH core, avoid illiquid alts for large positions

Emotional Risk

Panic selling, FOMO buying, checking daily

→ Managed by: written framework, pre-set rules, not checking daily
