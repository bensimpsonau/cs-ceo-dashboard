Conversion Optimisation

Title & Thumbnail Strategy

The right title converts HNW searchers into viewers. These patterns are proven to resonate with the 40–59 serious wealth builder archetype.

Title Patterns That Convert

Pattern 1 — The Honest Answer
"Is [Topic] Actually [Common Belief]? The Honest Answer" → e.g., Is Bitcoin Actually a Hedge Against Inflation? The Honest Answer
Pattern 2 — The Contrarian
"Why I'm [Contrarian Position] in a [Market Condition]" → e.g., Why I'm Buying Digital Assets in a Bear Market
Pattern 3 — The Smart Investor List
"The [#] Things [Smart/Successful] Investors Do When [Situation]" → e.g., The 5 Things Smart Investors Do When Prices Drop
Pattern 4 — The Experience Frame
"I've Been in Digital Assets [# Years]. Here's What Actually Matters." → builds credibility through lived experience
Pattern 5 — The System Frame
"The [Exact System/Framework] I Use to [Outcome]" → positions Ben as having a superior methodology
Pattern 6 — The Authority Endorsement
"Why [Famous/Credible Entity] Is Quietly [Action]" → e.g., What BlackRock Knows About Bitcoin That Most People Don't
Pattern 7 — The Macro Implication
"What [Big Event/Trend] Means for Your [Wealth/Portfolio]" → makes macro relevant to personal wealth
Title Language — NEVER USE

"crypto" · "moon" · "gains" · "returns" · "bull run" (in speculative context) · "100x" · "get rich"

Always include Ben's face with clear expression. Pair with dollar amounts or bold statement text.

If CTR underperforms at the 72-hour mark, update the thumbnail immediately. Test every upload.

Clean, premium look. NOT clickbait. NOT crypto-bro. Dark backgrounds, orange/white text — CS brand.

No "$$$" stacks. No rocket emojis. No shocked faces. Repels HNW ICP immediately.
