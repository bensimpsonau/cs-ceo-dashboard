Bitcoin Mining Tax Strategy

Competitive intel on Mark Moss / Market Disruptors. Feasibility for CS Platinum.

TL;DR

  • The play: Borrow against your BTC (not taxable), buy mining rigs, claim 100% bonus depreciation (US), stack mined BTC as income.
  • US: Works. 100% bonus depreciation is now permanent (One Big Beautiful Bill Act, July 2025). A $100K rig purchase = $100K Year 1 deduction = ~$40K tax savings at 40% bracket.
  • Australia: Doesn't work the same way. No 100% depreciation. Standard 25%/yr over 4 years. Division 35 blocks high earners from offsetting mining losses against salary. Investigating business structure workaround.
  • Mark Moss's model: High-ticket coaching ($5K-$25K+). Refers clients to Arch Lending (loans) + Blockware (mining). Earns referral commissions. Stays in "education" lane.
  • CS opportunity: Build a "Bitcoin Treasury Strategy" module inside Platinum. Partner with Arch + Blockware. Referral revenue + increased Platinum value. Strong ICP fit for US members.

How the Strategy Works

1

Pledge BTC

Borrow against your Bitcoin through Arch Lending. NOT a taxable event. LTV 10-50%. Interest rolled annually.

2

Buy Miners

Purchase ASICs (e.g. S21 Pro, ~$2,400 each) through Blockware. They host, manage, and run the hardware.

3

Claim Depreciation

US: 100% bonus depreciation in Year 1 (Section 168k). Deduct full cost against taxable income. AU: 25%/yr standard.

4

Stack BTC

Blockware sends mined BTC to your wallet monthly. Income is taxable at FMV when received.

5

Repay Loan

Use mining proceeds to repay the Arch loan over time. Keep excess BTC. Repeat as BTC appreciates.

6

Repeat

As BTC price rises, collateral grows. Borrow more. Buy more miners. "Perpetual income" loop.

Legal / AFSL Position

Bitcoin is NOT a "financial product" under Australian law (ASIC guidance). Basic education about BTC strategies does not require an AFSL.

Safe zone: "Here's how bonus depreciation works. Here's how BTC-backed lending works. Here's the math." - This is factual education.

Grey zone: "Mining combined with lending is an effective tax strategy for high earners." - General advice, likely safe with disclaimers.

Danger zone: "Based on YOUR income, you should borrow $X and buy Y miners." - Personal advice. Requires AFSL and/or tax agent registration.

Moss's approach: Teaches the "what" and "why." Partners handle the "how." Disclaimers everywhere. CS should mirror this model.

Key Risks

Hardware obsolescence

S21 Pro lifespan 2-4 years before newer models make it uncompetitive.

Regulatory change

US depreciation rules could change. AU crypto framework still evolving.
