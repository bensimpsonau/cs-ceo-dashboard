Ascension Model

Pro Accelerator — 90 Days

A 90-day entry tier with Research, Circle community, and introductory education. Experience the real product — then access is cut, creating urgency to upgrade to Platinum.

✓ Days 1–90 (Full Access)

  • Full Research access (weekly market updates, digital asset reports, CS Portfolio)
  • Attend B1 Foundations Session (same as Platinum L1)
  • 90 days of Beginner Weekly Calls (Tue) + Market Updates (Thu)
  • Start Phase 3 — begin the 8-Step Strategy process
  • 1:1 analyst session (starts their personal strategy)
  • Full community access (Beginner's Lounge, General, Ask The Analyst)
  • All pre-recorded L1 education modules

✕ After 90 Days (Revoked)

  • No more weekly calls
  • No more community access
  • No portfolio or research access
  • No 1:1 analyst sessions
  • Cannot complete Phase 3 (strategy is unfinished)

✓ KEEPS: Pre-recorded education modules only
