Progression

L1 → L2 Graduation

The gate exists to protect members. Going too deep too fast is how people lose money. Graduation is earned, not timed.

✅ Gating Requirements

1
Complete B1 Foundations session
2-hour live session covering Steps 1–4
2
Complete all B2 How-To modules
Steps 5–8 via weekly coaching calls
3
Complete B3 Strategy Workbook
Full 8-step strategy documented
4
1:1 Analyst Session
Analyst signs off on strategy readiness

Module tick-off, not tests. Completion-based, not exam-based. Dee confirmed this is the right model — members want progress tracking, not pressure.

🔓 What Unlocks at Level 2

The moment a member graduates, their entire dashboard changes. New content. New capabilities. New confidence.

💰 Yield & Staking
DeFi yields, stablecoin strategies, staking mechanics
🏦 Borrowing
Collateralised lending, liquidation mechanics, Ledn/Unchained
🔐 Multi-Sig
Advanced custody, estate planning, institutional-grade
📋 Entity Structures
SMSF, trusts, company holdings, cross-jurisdiction

⏱️ Typical Timeline

~90 days for most members. Some faster (experienced investors who just needed a system), some slower (complete beginners). The gate isn't time-based — it's completion-based. Dee suggested 90–180 days as realistic.

Team Standards

Key Language & Rules

Every team member — analyst, CSM, content creator, marketer — must use consistent language. These are non-negotiable.

✅
"Digital assets"

Always say "digital assets" — never "crypto" or "cryptocurrency". We are a digital asset education company.

✅
Bitcoin stays "Bitcoin"

Bitcoin is always capitalised, always separate from "altcoins." Bitcoin is its own asset class. Never group it with altcoins.

✅
Circle, not WhatsApp

Community lives on Circle. Structured, searchable, professional. WhatsApp is being migrated away from.

✅
Education company, not advisory

"We are an education company" — never "advisors", "financial advice", or "signals". We teach frameworks.

✅
"We teach you how to think, not what to think"

The signature tagline. Use it in content, calls, onboarding, everywhere. It's who we are.

✅
Frameworks, not signals

"Here's how we think about it" — never "buy this" or "sell now". Members build their OWN strategy.

The Shift OS Philosophy

Core Belief

"A strategy in your head is not a strategy. It's a vague intention."

Win/Win Model

We invest alongside members. No ads. No token promotions. Our success = their success.

Self-Sovereignty

Members own their strategy. We give the frameworks and research. They make every decision.
