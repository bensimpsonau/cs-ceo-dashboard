Issues List (EOS-Style Discussion)

Identify, Discuss, Solve. These are the open issues for today's session.

  • 1
    Cash gap if raise doesn't close
    What is the contingency plan if the A$3M pre-Series A doesn't close by end of month? At current burn (A$336K/mo overhead), runway is less than one month. Even with Phase 3 restructure, overhead (~A$275K) still exceeds non-paid revenue contribution (A$162K) by A$112K/mo.
  • 2
    ROAS at breakeven on paid - when to scale or cut?
    Paid channel is at 1.84x ROAS. Not contributing to profit. Do we maintain current spend, reduce, or cut entirely while we build organic?
  • 3
    Team morale during restructure
    How do we maintain team confidence and performance while making significant structural changes? Communication plan needed for remaining team.
  • 4
    Matt review timing (30-60 days)
    Phase 3 is the biggest savings tranche. When exactly do we make the call? What metrics determine the outcome?
  • 5
    US expansion timeline vs cash reality
    US sales team training is a Q2 rock, but requires investment. Does cash position support this, or do we delay until post-restructure savings are realized?
