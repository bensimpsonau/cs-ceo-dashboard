1. Who They Are UPDATED

"A time-poor professional, business owner, or family investor aged 40+ with $25K-$1M+ they want allocated into digital assets properly - who wants a trusted team doing the research so they don't have to gamble on YouTube, Twitter, and a mate's tip."

Key shift from v3.0: Removed "done-for-you" framing. CS does NOT manage money or make investment decisions for members. CS provides research, frameworks, community, and education. The feel is that someone has your back. The reality is you still make every decision yourself.

The 3 Buyer Profiles UPDATED

Percentages adjusted to reflect Cohort 3 (aspirational target) rather than current member mix. First-timers are a much larger addressable market than our current base suggests.

Profile 1: Dan the First-Timer 40% of Cohort 3

Was 29% in v3.0. This is the largest segment of our aspirational audience - people we haven't captured yet.

Who: New to digital assets. Thinking about it for years. Hasn't pulled the trigger. 45-60, professional/retired.

Capital: $25K-$100K ready. Net worth $250K-$1M+.

Core fear: Making an expensive first mistake.

Language: "I don't know who to trust." "I don't know what to actually buy." "I don't want to get this wrong."

What converts: Trust signals, newbie-friendly testimonials, "team guiding every move" promise, Foundations Track.

Do NOT use: HNW language, institutional-grade, sophisticated investor, "done-for-you."

Key testimonial - Ted: Mortgage broker, San Diego. Zero crypto. "They're literally here just to help the members."
Profile 2: Dan the Overwhelmed 35% of Cohort 3

Was 46% in v3.0. Still the largest single segment of current members, but Cohort 3 skews more to first-timers.

Who: Has some exposure but feels lost. Business owner, senior professional. 40-59.

Capital: $50K-$500K+ ready. Net worth $500K-$2M+.

Core fear: Information overload, no strategy, no time. Research confidence: 3.7/10.

Language: "Cut through the noise." "I need a structured plan." "Know when to take profits."

What converts: Ship list, exit framework, "1 hour a week" promise, having a team backing them.

Key testimonial - Alex: $2M+ in crypto. "Losing money is more expensive than the membership."
Profile 3: Dan the Burned 25% of Cohort 3

Unchanged from v3.0.

Who: In digital assets 4+ years. Watched gains evaporate 2021-2022. May have lost on Voyager/FTX.

Capital: $50K-$250K+ existing, $25K-$250K+ more. Net worth $500K-$2M+.

Core fear: Round-tripping gains again. Exit confidence: 2.8/10.

Language: "I should have had a team from day one." "I need to know when to take profits."

What converts: Exit framework, 116% CAGR, Eric's testimonial, Ben's real story.

Key testimonial - Sophie: Couple. "You can either pay for help or pay for mistakes. Pick your poison."

Why Cohort 3 percentages differ: v3.0 reflected current members (Overwhelmed + Burned = 71%). Cohort 3 - the aspirational audience we're targeting - skews heavily to first-timers. The repositioned funnel now speaks to them directly, and bear market timing creates a natural entry point. Travis: "If you look at cohort three, more of them are new to market than anything else."

The "Done-for-you Feel" Framework NEW LEGAL

Travis's clarification: It's never "done for you" - it's "done for you feel."

✅ Write it like this

  • "A team doing the research so you don't have to"
  • "Your research team. Your frameworks. Your decisions."
  • "Everything you need to make confident decisions"
  • "The research is done. The frameworks are built. You follow the system."

❌ Never write this

  • "Done-for-you investing"
  • "We tell you what to buy and sell"
  • "We manage your portfolio"
  • "We handle everything"
