How Gross Margin Is Calculated

Commission is paid on gross margin — not top-line revenue. Gross margin accounts for refunds, ad spend (paid channel only), and sales team commissions. Here's how it works by channel:

Renewals (A$150K/month) are excluded from Alchemy commission. Renewal revenue is retained by CS to cover core OPEX. Alchemy is commissionable on organic new sales and paid new sales only.
