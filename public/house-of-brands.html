01 / Why a House of Brands

Our ICP is a time-poor professional aged 40-59, $1M+ net worth, whose deepest fear is making a catastrophic financial mistake. At any given moment they exist in one of several psychological states — searching for authority, wanting proof, stuck in hesitation, or needing a safe starting point. One voice cannot reach all of these states. Five angles can. Each runs in parallel, catching the ICP wherever they are right now.

The Strategic Wealth Builder does not move through a neat funnel. They bounce between psychological states — confident one week, paralysed the next. Our job is to have the right voice active for whichever state they are in right now. All five angles run simultaneously. The ICP enters through whichever one matches their current mindset, and every angle ultimately leads to the same place: Platinum.

04 / Chemicals of Money — Content Series

Four-part series by Travis. One chemical per post. LinkedIn + Instagram. Tone: research-backed, empathetic, never preachy. Each piece ends with a practical reframe, not a sales pitch.

😰 Fear — Cortisol + Adrenaline
When markets drop, your amygdala hijacks your prefrontal cortex. You're in survival mode. This is biology, not stupidity.
Content angle: "Bitcoin dropped 48%. Your cortisol spiked. That's not weakness — it's 200,000 years of evolution."
😤 Ego — Testosterone + Serotonin
Up 300%? Feeling untouchable? That conviction has nothing to do with data. More portfolios are destroyed at tops than bottoms.
Content angle: "The most dangerous version of you as an investor is the one who is winning."
💰 Greed — Dopamine
Every green candle is a dopamine hit. Your brain wants more. Same mechanism as gambling addiction.
Content angle: "Checking your portfolio 12x a day is not due diligence. It's a dopamine loop."
🧘 Clarity — GABA
The brain's inhibitory neurotransmitter. Calm, second-order thinking. The best investors have access to this when fear-driven ones don't.
Content angle: "The investors who win during extreme markets aren't smarter. They have more GABA."
