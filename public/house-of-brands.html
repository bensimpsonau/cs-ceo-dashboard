01 / Why a House of Brands

Our ICP is a time-poor professional aged 40-59, $1M+ net worth, driven by a clear goal: protect and grow generational wealth on their own terms, with a framework they trust. They have built significant wealth through discipline and data. Their fear is not poverty — it's making a preventable mistake that sets them back. Goals drive them. Fear is the edge they want removed. At any given moment they exist in one of several psychological states — searching for authority, wanting proof, needing a plan, or looking for a safe starting point. One voice cannot reach all of these states. Five angles can. Each runs in parallel, catching the ICP wherever they are right now.

The Strategic Wealth Builder does not move through a neat funnel. They bounce between psychological states — confident one week, paralysed the next. Our job is to have the right voice active for whichever state they are in right now. All five angles run simultaneously. The ICP enters through whichever one matches their current mindset, and every angle ultimately leads to the same place: Platinum.

08 / Top-of-Funnel Pre-Framing Content Plan

These are 90-second to 2-minute videos that run above the funnel. Their only job: shift a completely unaware HNW viewer from "digital assets are a scam" to "I need to seriously consider this." Not about Collective Shift. Not a pitch. Pure education that ends on an open loop and drives to the quiz. Four content buckets, three personas, 12 scripts in rotation.

TOFU Goal
Move the ICP from Unaware to Problem Aware and create enough tension that they opt in to find out what to do about it.
The Bridge
Video open loop CTA: "free wealth assessment" → quiz → name/email/mobile captured → Blueprint delivered → setter calls within 2 hours
Content Volume
1 bucket per week, rotating. 4-6 hook variations per bucket. 16-24 TOFU pieces per quarter across 3 personas.
