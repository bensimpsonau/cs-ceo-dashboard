Strategic Overview Jeremy Haynes Framework
Three distinct voices. One unified mission: drive 35 Platinum memberships per month ($700K/month). Every piece of content feeds into the Platinum funnel through two parallel ad tracks: Hammer Them (content consumption, no CTA) and Direct Response (book a call). The House of Brands is the content engine that makes both tracks work.

Jeremy's Forester Framework (Skill #7): Run content consumption ads (Video Views objective) alongside direct response ads. 30-50% of total ad budget goes to "Hammer Them" content. This warms cold audiences cheaply, creates the swarm effect (ICP sees Ben everywhere), and transforms show rates from 50% to 60-78%. Content ads and direct response ads must run as SEPARATE campaigns with SEPARATE budgets. Mixing them confuses Meta's algorithm.
Jeremy's Hidden VSSL Framework (Skill #14): Every persona creates content that sells without triggering "this person is selling me" resistance. Structure: genuine education that naturally leads to "...and if you want us to build this for you, that's what Platinum is." HNW investors are allergic to hard sells. No value stacks. No hype. The content IS the sell. The call is just the next logical step.
Two-Track Ad Architecture Forester / Hammer Them
Every persona's content feeds into two parallel advertising tracks. These must be separate campaigns in Meta with separate objectives and budgets. This is non-negotiable.

Track 1: Ben Simpson Organic

HAMMER THEM (Content Consumption)
  • Objective: Video Views / ThruPlay
  • Budget: 30% of total ad spend (~$21-24K/month)
  • CTA: None. Pure content. Build familiarity.
  • Content: Podcast clips, YouTube reels, testimonials, market commentary, Platinum day highlights
  • Goal: Build custom audiences from 25%, 50%, 75% video viewers
  • Target CPV: $0.25-$0.50 per retargetable view
  • Key metric: audience size growth + cost per ThruPlay

Track 2: Ben Simpson Education

DIRECT RESPONSE (Book a Call)
  • Objective: Conversions (call booking)
  • Budget: 70% of total ad spend (~$49-58K/month)
  • CTA: "Book a portfolio review call"
  • Content: VSL, lead magnet ads, retargeting warm audiences from Track 1
  • Price stated: $14,999 in VSL (pixel conditioning)
  • Target: $600-700 cost per booked call
  • Key metric: calls booked, show rate, close rate
How the Two Tracks Connect: Track 1 (Hammer Them) builds warm audiences from cold traffic. Track 2 (DR) retargets those warm audiences with direct response ads. The ICP sees Ben's content 5-10 times before they ever see a "book a call" ad. By the time they do, they feel like they already know him. This is why show rates hit 60-78% instead of 40-50%.
