How This System Works

Four individuals. One master brand. Every piece of content serves both.

The Core Principle

People discover Travis on YouTube, find Sarn's tutorial on wallets, or see Matt's data thread on X. Each touchpoint routes them into the Collective Shift ecosystem. The individual builds trust. The brand converts trust into membership.

Content Attribution

Every piece of personal content includes the CS connection. Format: "[Name], [Role] @collectiveshift"

  • Direct-to-camera reels: published on personal account, CS reposts best performers
  • Research and analysis: published on CS YouTube + that person's X/social
  • Tutorials: published on CS YouTube + Sarn's own channel
  • All CTAs route to collectiveshift.co/cryptoblueprint or ManyChat keyword triggers

Cross-Pollination

  • Matt's research becomes the data backbone for Ben's reels and Friday email open loops
  • Sarn's tutorials get clipped into beginner-friendly IG carousels for the CS brand account
  • Travis's coaching frameworks provide the psychology angle for Wednesday story emails
  • Ben's personal stories get expanded into LinkedIn long-form and podcast episodes
  • Podcast conversations produce 6+ short-form clips per episode

Ben Simpson

Founder & CEO
The face, founder, and beating heart of Collective Shift.

A. Bio & Brand Positioning

Positioning Statement

Ben Simpson is the conviction engine of Collective Shift. He is the founder who dropped out of high school, went bankrupt twice, lived above a 7-Eleven in Bali, and built one of the world's leading digital asset education companies from a $49/month Facebook group. His vulnerability is his superpower. His track record is his proof.

Full Bio

Ben Simpson discovered Bitcoin at 17 when a friend showed him peer-to-peer transfers could bypass the banking headaches he was hitting with his sportswear import business. He went all in. Made hundreds of thousands on paper. Then round-tripped the entire thing. Twice.

The second crash nearly ended everything. He was living above a 7-Eleven in Bali because he couldn't afford anywhere else. His bookkeeper told him they had weeks of cash left. No one was coming to save him.

Through it all, Ben learned the hard way that influencers, theories, and "vibes and hope" don't work. What works: independent research, fundamentals over technicals, a system, and an exit strategy. He partnered with Matt Willemsen (now Director of Research), turned down every affiliate and sponsor offer, and built Collective Shift from scratch.

Today, Collective Shift serves 1,000+ members worldwide with a 116% CAGR over six financial years (audited by Crypto Tax Australia). Ben is a TEDx speaker, international keynote presenter, and has an online audience of 150,000+. He invests his own capital alongside every member. If the research is wrong, he loses money too.

His biggest goal was never a Lamborghini or a Rolex. It was buying his parents a house. He did that in 2026.

What Makes Ben Credible

  • 9+ years as a digital asset investor (ETH at $80, BTC in low thousands)
  • 116% CAGR over 6 financial years, audited by Crypto Tax Australia
  • Built CS from $49/month to seven figures per month in revenue
  • TEDx speaker and international keynote presenter
  • 150,000+ online audience across all platforms
  • Zero affiliates, zero token promotions since founding
  • Invests own capital alongside every single member

B. Voice & Tone Profile

Voice DNA: 5 Traits

  1. Vulnerable + Convicted. Shares real failures alongside real wins. Never pretends to have had it easy. But absolute conviction in the path forward.
  2. Direct and Australian. No corporate speak. Says it straight. Uses "reckon", "mate", natural Australian cadence. Feels like a conversation at a barbecue with a very smart friend.
  3. Contrarian. Challenges mainstream narratives. "Everyone's saying X. Here's why I think they're wrong." This is Ben's signature move.
  4. Personal storyteller. Ben's personal stories get 50x the engagement of pure education. The origin story, family moments, the near-bankruptcy stories. Always lead with story.
  5. Educator, not advisor. "I'll teach you how to think, not what to think." Never gives financial advice. Gives frameworks.

Signature Phrases

  • "Don't round trip. It's so painful. Have a strategy. Have a sale plan."
  • "We only get paid for our research. If we're bad, our clients don't make money."
  • "Bitcoin is the best savings technology ever created."
  • "Money is great, but freedom is the real goal."
  • "I've made every mistake so you don't have to."

Do This

  • "I went bankrupt twice before I figured out what actually works in this market."
  • "Everyone's watching the price. Almost nobody is watching the on-chain data. Here's what it shows."
  • "My parents still rent. That's why I started this. I wanted to change my family's trajectory."
  • "We've turned down hundreds of thousands from exchanges wanting us to promote their products."
  • "Here's the one framework that changed how I think about taking profits."

Not That

  • "In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape..." (corporate jargon)
  • "BTC to $500K by December!" (price predictions / hype)
  • "This altcoin is about to 100x" (speculative shilling)
  • "Use code BEN10 for 10% off at Exchange X" (affiliate language)
  • "Act now before it's too late!" (fake urgency / scarcity tactics)

Voice Calibration

  • Formality: 3/10. Casual, warm, conversational. Never stiff.
  • Technical depth: 5/10. Understands deeply but explains simply. Lets Matt handle the deep data.
  • Humour: Dry, self-deprecating. "I dropped out of high school and somehow ended up speaking at TEDx."
  • Emotion: High. Ben wears his heart on his sleeve. That's the differentiator.

Positioning Statement

Travis Thomas is the operating system builder of Collective Shift. Where Ben provides the conviction and Matt provides the data, Travis provides the framework that turns knowledge into action. Full Bio

Travis Thomas is the Chief Growth Officer and Head Coach at Collective Shift. He sits at the intersection of investor psychology and wealth architecture, two disciplines that most people in digital assets completely ignore.

Most investors know what to buy. Very few know how to hold it through a 40% drawdown, when to take profits, or how to build a portfolio that actually matches their life goals. That's the gap Travis fills. He owns the "operating system" angle at Collective Shift: how goal-driven investors use frameworks like position sizing, game theory, and structured exit plans to build generational wealth.

Before Collective Shift, Travis built his career in growth strategy and high-performance coaching. He understands what makes people stick to a plan and, more importantly, what makes them abandon one. That psychology background is what makes his coaching different. He doesn't just hand you a spreadsheet. He builds the mental model that keeps you executing when the market is screaming at you to panic.

At CS, Travis leads Platinum member coaching, manages the product division (overseeing research and education), and drives the company's growth strategy. He works directly with investors managing $100K to $5M+ in digital assets.

What Makes Travis Credible

  • Chief Growth Officer overseeing Product, Platinum Success, and Research divisions
  • Specialises in wealth goal architecture and investor psychology
  • Coaches HNW investors ($100K-$5M+) through volatile markets
  • Owns the "operating system" framework that forms CS's coaching methodology
  • Background in growth strategy and high-performance coaching
  • Works daily with real investors facing real portfolio decisions

B. Voice & Tone Profile

Voice DNA: 5 Traits

  1. Coaching mode first. Every post is a mini coaching session. Asks questions, guides thinking, leads to an insight. Never lectures.
  2. Structured delivery. Loves numbered steps, frameworks, acronyms. Makes complex psychology actionable. "Here are the 3 questions to ask yourself before any trade."
  3. Results-oriented. Always connects the psychology to the portfolio outcome. "This is why it matters for your returns."
  4. Energetic but grounded. Motivating without being "motivational speaker". Data-backed energy. Grounded enthusiasm, not hype.
  5. HNW-aware. Speaks to people managing $500K+. Respects their sophistication. Never condescending. Peer-to-peer energy.

Signature Phrases

  • "Let's build your operating system for this market."
  • "Your portfolio doesn't need more information. It needs a better framework."
  • "The difference between a good investor and a great one isn't knowledge. It's process."
  • "Goals first. Allocation second. Everything else is noise."
  • "What's your exit plan? If you don't have one, that's the first thing we fix."

Do This

  • "Here are the 3 questions I ask every investor before we touch their portfolio."
  • "Your allocation should match your goals, not your emotions. Here's how to check."
  • "I coached someone this week who had $2M in property and zero digital assets. We built a plan in 30 minutes."
  • "Position sizing isn't sexy. But it's the reason our members sleep at night."
  • "Before the next move, ask yourself: does this serve my 5-year goal or my 5-minute feeling?"

Not That

  • "WAGMI! LFG! To the moon!" (hype language)
  • "BTC is going to $200K this cycle" (price predictions are Ben/Matt territory)
  • "Just DCA and hold forever" (oversimplified advice)
  • Generic motivational quotes with sunset backgrounds
  • Competing with Ben on macro takes or conviction content

Voice Calibration

  • Formality: 4/10. Professional but warm. Coach energy, not corporate energy.
  • Technical depth: 4/10. Knows the fundamentals well but his value is frameworks and psychology, not data.
  • Humour: Light, situational. Uses relatable investor scenarios to get a chuckle. "We've all panic-sold at 2am. Let's fix that."
  • Emotion: Controlled energy. Motivating without melodrama. "You can do this" without the cringe.

How Travis Complements Ben

Ben provides the why (conviction, vision, personal proof). Travis provides the how (frameworks, process, coaching). Together they form a complete investor journey: Ben inspires action, Travis builds the system to sustain it. They should never compete for the same content lane.
