TOPIC 1

Advanced Tax Optimisation — Minimising Your Legal Tax Burden

Tax-Loss Harvesting Strategies

What it is: selling losing positions to crystallise capital losses, then using those losses to offset capital gains. Timing: typically done before end of financial year (June 30 in Australia). Process: identify positions trading below your cost basis → sell → book the loss → optionally re-enter after a suitable period. Important: wash sale rules vary by jurisdiction. Australia currently has no explicit wash sale rule for digital assets (as of 2026), but the ATO may apply the general anti-avoidance rule. US: wash sale rules now apply to digital assets from 2025.

CGT Discount Optimisation

The 12-month rule (Australia): hold an asset for >12 months and receive a 50% CGT discount. Strategy: timing sell decisions around the 12-month mark. Example: if you bought BTC 11 months ago and want to sell, waiting 1 month saves you 50% of the CGT. Planning purchases with the 12-month discount in mind. How to track holding periods in your tax software.

DeFi-Specific Tax Events

Staking rewards: generally treated as income at the time of receipt (Australia). Yield/interest from DeFi lending: income at receipt. Liquidity provision entry/exit: potential CGT event (swapping tokens). Airdrops: income at time of receipt based on fair market value. Gas fees: deductible as a cost of acquisition or disposal. How to track DeFi transactions for tax purposes (Koinly DeFi integration, DeBank).

Accounting for Gas Fees and Transaction Costs

Gas fees on purchase: add to cost base (reduces future CGT). Gas fees on sale: deduct from sale proceeds (reduces CGT). Gas fees for DeFi interactions: deductible as investment management expense. Record keeping: ensure your tax software captures gas fees automatically.

TOPIC 2

Entity Structures for Tax — Corporate and Trust Holdings

This topic connects directly to Module 4 (Multi-Entity Structures). Here we focus on the tax implications and optimisation of each structure.

SMSF Digital Asset Holdings

Tax rates: 15% on gains in accumulation phase, 0% in pension phase. Setup: SMSF must have a compliant investment strategy that includes digital assets. Custody: must use a regulated custodian or have an audit trail for self-custody. Compliance: annual audit, investment strategy review, sole purpose test. Cost: $2K-$5K setup, $2K-$4K annual compliance. When it makes sense: long-term holders with $200K+ in super who want digital asset exposure. Warning: SMSF rules are strict — compliance failure can result in taxing the entire fund at 45%.

Family Trust Structures

How trusts work: income and gains are distributed to beneficiaries in lower tax brackets. Digital assets in a trust: the trustee manages the portfolio, distributions are flexible. Benefits: asset protection, income splitting, estate planning simplification. Costs: $2K-$3K setup, $1.5K-$3K annual accounting. When it makes sense: high-income earners ($180K+) with family members in lower tax brackets.

Company vs Personal Holdings

Company tax rate: 25% (base rate entity) vs personal marginal rates (up to 47%). Companies: no 50% CGT discount, but consistent lower rate. Best for: active traders who want a flat tax rate and full deductibility. Not ideal for: long-term holders who benefit from the 50% CGT discount at personal level. The calculation: if your marginal rate is >25% AND you trade frequently, company structure may be beneficial.

Cross-Jurisdiction Considerations

🇺🇸 USA: IRS Form 8949, wash sale rules apply from 2025, long-term vs short-term capital gains distinction. 🇳🇿 New Zealand: digital assets taxed as property, no CGT but income tax may apply. 🇬🇧 UK: CGT with £6K annual exemption (2024/25). 🇪🇺 varies by country. Key message: international members MUST get advice in their specific jurisdiction. CS provides frameworks, not jurisdiction-specific tax advice.

When to Consult a Specialist Digital Asset Accountant

Always for: entity structure decisions, SMSF setup, cross-border holdings. Recommended for: portfolios >$50K, active DeFi users, members with staking/yield income. How to find one: CS can recommend digital asset-specialist accountants in Australia. The conversation: bring your tax software report (Koinly/CTC), your entity structure question, and your investment strategy.
