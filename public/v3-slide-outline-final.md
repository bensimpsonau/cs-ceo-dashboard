Decentralisation. Fixed supply. Network effect. Lindy effect. Institutional adoption (ETFs, sovereign wealth, corporate treasuries).

### Slide 11 — Long-Term Bitcoin Stack
Accumulation > trading. DCA. 200-week MA as fair value floor. 4-year cycle. Never lower than 4 years prior.

### Slide 12 — When Should We Buy BTC?
"I will buy at X" framework. Write your number BEFORE fear hits. 30% drops are normal.

### Slide 13 — The Feeling of Fear
Best buying opportunities feel terrible. Emotional cycle at play.

### Slide 14 — The Confidence Scale
Rate conviction 1-10. What changes it? Build your framework now, not in a panic.

### Slide 15 — 🎯 ACTION: Your BTC Plan
BTC allocation target. Buy prices. DCA plan. Conviction level. Exit criteria.
