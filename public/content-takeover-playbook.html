Step 1: Script Creation

Marketing Bot drafts podcast scripts, talking points, and episode outlines based on content calendar and SEO Bot keyword research. Analyst Bot fact-checks all data points, prices, statistics, and claims before script is finalised. Scripts stored in Notion.

Step 2: Recording

Ben, Travis, or Sarn records on Riverside. High-quality audio/video capture. Riverside generates automatic transcript for downstream processing.

Step 3: Post-Production

Riverside AI handles: Magic Audio (noise removal, leveling), Magic Clips (auto-generated short clips), Co-Creator (titles, descriptions), text-based editing. Giorgio edits long-form. Fiverr editor polishes short-form clips.

Step 4: Packaging

Marketing Bot creates: YouTube titles, descriptions, tags, chapters, Anchor/Spotify descriptions, thumbnail concepts (sent to Fiverr designer), email newsletter copy, blog post summaries.

Step 5: Distribution

Social Bot creates platform-specific posts: YouTube community posts, Instagram captions + hashtags, TikTok descriptions, X/Twitter threads, LinkedIn articles. Email Bot drafts episode newsletters and promo emails.

Step 6: Upload + Publish

VA handles final uploads to all platforms. Scheduling coordinated via Asana board (7 columns: Backlog > Scripting > Recording > Editing > Review > Scheduled > Published). BenBot tracks progress and follows up on deadlines.

Tools powering the pipeline: Riverside (recording + AI), Asana (project management), Slack (contractor coordination), Google Drive (file storage by brand/month/episode), Notion (scripts + planning)
