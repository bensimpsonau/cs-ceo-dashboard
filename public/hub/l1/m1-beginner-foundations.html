STEP 01 B1 — FOUNDATIONS SESSION WEEK 1

Beginner Foundations

The entry point for every member. Establishes why digital assets matter, how markets move in cycles, and why multiple blockchain ecosystems exist. Sets the mental framework for everything that follows.

Delivery & Timing

📅 Week 1 ⏱ ~30 mins (of 2-hour session) 🎤 Live 2-hour Foundations Session 📹 Recorded session + PDF

Live delivery: First section of the 2-hour B1 Foundations Session (~30 mins). Covers Steps 1–4 in a single live session with 40–50 slides.

Self-paced: Recorded session available after live delivery + module summary PDF for reference.

CORE THESIS

Why Digital Assets Exist

The core thesis — why this asset class exists and why it matters for every portfolio.

  • Store of value, decentralisation, financial sovereignty — the shift from traditional finance
  • What makes Bitcoin different from every previous monetary technology
  • The macro case for holding digital assets in a traditional portfolio

"No one makes it in a single cycle. Stick it out."

— Teaching Framework #7: One Cycle Is Not Enough

Key Questions Answered

  • "Why should I be here?"
  • "Is this real or just hype?"
MARKET CYCLES HIGH IMPACT

Market Cycles — What History Tells Us

Understanding the cyclical nature of digital asset markets — the pattern that repeats and why it matters for your journey.

  • Historical bull/bear transitions — the 4-year cycle theory
  • How drawdowns have played out (2014, 2018, 2022) — what "normal" looks like
  • Why bear markets are the best time to accumulate
  • Reading where we are in the current cycle

"No one makes it in a single cycle. Stick it out."

— Teaching Framework #7: One Cycle Is Not Enough

Key Questions Answered

  • "When will the market recover?"
  • "Is this a bear market?"
  • "Have we seen this before?"
👥 Member Evidence

New members (Nigel, Stacey, Greg) have zero reference for bear/bull transitions. This is completely new territory for them — they've never experienced a full cycle. This section is critical for setting realistic expectations.

ECOSYSTEMS

Blockchain Ecosystems — Why There Are Many Chains

Understanding that different blockchains are different ecosystems — not all "the same thing."

  • Bitcoin chain vs Ethereum vs Solana as separate ecosystems
  • Why different chains exist and how they relate to each other
  • Layer 1 vs Layer 2 basics
  • The "tech startups" mental model for understanding ecosystems

Key Questions Answered

  • "What's the difference between Bitcoin and Ethereum?"
  • "Why are there so many chains?"
👥 Member Evidence

Members don't understand Bitcoin chain vs Solana vs Ethereum as separate ecosystems. They conflate all digital assets as "the same thing." This section provides the critical mental model that everything else builds on.

✅ Member Outcomes

After completing this module, members can:

✓Understand WHY digital assets exist and the investment thesis
✓Recognise where we are in the current market cycle
✓Understand that different blockchains are different ecosystems (not all "digital assets")
✓Have realistic expectations — this is a multi-cycle journey

🧠 Teaching Frameworks Used in This Module

#7 — "One Cycle Is Not Enough"

"No one makes it in a single cycle. Stick it out."

Use in: Market cycles content, retention messaging during drawdowns, Foundations session opening.

Why it works: Sets expectations. Reduces churn during drawdowns. Members who internalise this stay longer.

📋 Instructor Delivery Notes

Session format: This is the FIRST section of the 2-hour B1 Foundations Session. Approximately 30 minutes. 40–50 slides total across all 4 steps.

Audience: Complete beginners to digital assets, or experienced investors who lack a structured system. Zero assumptions about prior knowledge.

Tone: Reassuring but honest. Many members are anxious — they've heard about digital assets but feel overwhelmed. Start with the "why" before getting into any specifics.

Key pivot point: The ecosystems section is where you plant the "tech startups" mental model that gets expanded in Step 3 Altcoins. Make sure this lands — it's the foundation for everything that follows about altcoins.

CEX only rule: 🚫 No yield, no staking, no DeFi, no borrowing content in Level 1. Keep it strictly CEX-based.

Language: Use "digital assets" not "crypto." Bitcoin stays as "Bitcoin." Community is on Circle (not WhatsApp).
