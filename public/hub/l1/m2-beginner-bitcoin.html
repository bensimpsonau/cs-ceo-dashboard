Beginner Bitcoin

Builds deep Bitcoin conviction through first-principles thinking. Teaches members why Bitcoin is fundamentally different from every other digital asset, and how to build a position with confidence.

CORE THESIS HIGH IMPACT

The Bitcoin Thesis — Why Bitcoin Is Different

First-principles case for why Bitcoin is fundamentally different from every other digital asset.

  • Scarcity (21M cap), decentralisation, network effects — the trifecta that makes Bitcoin unique
  • Store of value vs medium of exchange — Bitcoin as digital gold
  • Why long-term holders outperform — the data on holding through cycles

"Everyone gets Bitcoin at the price they deserve. That's the cost of entry."

— Teaching Framework #4: The Price You Deserve

Key Questions Answered

  • "Is it too late to buy Bitcoin?"
  • "Will it keep going up?"
CONVICTION

Bitcoin Conviction Building — The Long-Term View

Building educated conviction through data and first principles — not blind faith or influencer hype.

  • 5-year and 10-year price outlook — zoomed-out perspective beyond daily noise
  • Production cost analysis and what it means for price floors
  • Why long-term holders consistently outperform traders — the data speaks

"If someone put a gun to my head — where's Bitcoin in 5-10 years? About $500,000. That's 6x from here. Still way better than the bank."

— Teaching Framework #6: The 5-Year Gun-to-Head Test

Key Questions Answered

  • "Where will Bitcoin be in 5 years?"
  • "Should I sell my Bitcoin?"
👥 Member Evidence

Stacey wants to own 1 whole Bitcoin. Multiple members asking about price targets. This section builds the conviction they need to commit to a long-term accumulation plan rather than trying to time the market.

DCA STRATEGY MOST ASKED

DCA Entry Strategy — How to Build Your Position

The practical framework for building a Bitcoin position without emotion or timing anxiety.

  • Dollar cost averaging explained: why DCA removes emotion from buying decisions
  • Practical DCA amounts by portfolio size and risk profile
  • Lump sum vs DCA decision framework — when each approach makes sense
  • Frequency considerations: weekly vs fortnightly vs monthly

"Dollar cost averaging — you catch some up there, you catch some down there, you take the emotion out of it. I've never regretted DCA."

— Teaching Framework #2: The DCA Certainty

Key Questions Answered

  • "Should I buy now or wait?"
  • "How much per week/month?"
  • "Do I start now or wait for a dip?"
👥 Member Evidence

Asked in virtually every single call. Nigel: "Do I start buying a thousand dollars of Bitcoin each week, or do I wait another month?" This is the #1 recurring question — DCA is the answer that removes the paralysis.

CORE PHILOSOPHY

Bitcoin: Hold. Altcoins: Trade.

The clearest articulation of the CS investment philosophy — the single most important rule for portfolio construction.

  • Why Bitcoin is a hold-forever asset — data on long-term holders who never regret
  • Why this rule doesn't apply to altcoins — fundamentally different asset class
  • CS's actual sell history on Bitcoin — very rare, and for specific structural reasons

"I've never met a single person who's ever said to me: Simon, buying and holding Bitcoin for long term was a bad idea. Everyone who's been in the space long enough says: buying and holding an altcoin for long term was a bad idea."

— Teaching Framework #10: Bitcoin: Hold. Altcoins: Trade.

Key Questions Answered

  • "Do you ever sell Bitcoin?"
  • "Should I take profits on Bitcoin?"

📋 Instructor Delivery Notes

Session format: This is the SECOND section of the 2-hour B1 Foundations Session. Approximately 30 minutes.

Audience: Members who just completed Step 1 Foundations. They now understand why digital assets exist — this section builds conviction specifically around Bitcoin.

Tone: Confident but not preachy. Let the data do the talking. The conviction needs to be earned through understanding, not cheerleading.

Key pivot point: The DCA section is where theory meets action. This is the moment members go from "I understand Bitcoin" to "I'm ready to start buying." Make the practical steps crystal clear.

Most common question: "Should I buy now or wait?" — answered directly in the DCA Entry Strategy topic. Expect this in every session.

CEX only rule: 🚫 No yield, no staking, no DeFi, no borrowing content in Level 1. Keep it strictly CEX-based.

Language: Use "digital assets" not "crypto." Bitcoin stays as "Bitcoin." Community is on Circle (not WhatsApp).
