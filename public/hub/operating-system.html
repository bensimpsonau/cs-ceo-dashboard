Two-Track Education System

All 8 modules have Level 1 and Level 2 content. L1 is sequential — finish Phase 2 how-tos before Phase 3 strategy. L2 is concurrent — Phase 2 + Phase 3 run in parallel from Week 2.

📅

ONE weekly coaching call covers both phases — not separate calls. In L1 Weeks 2–5, the call focuses on that week's how-to module. From Week 5+ it shifts to supporting strategy work. For L2, the single call wraps both advanced how-tos AND strategy simultaneously.

🟢

Level 1 — Beginner Track

Sequential · 90 days · CEX only · No yield · No DeFi

B1 — Foundations Session · Week 1

One-off 2-hour live session covering Steps 1–4: Foundations, Bitcoin, Altcoins, How We Invest

B2 — Practical How-Tos · Weeks 2–5

One module per week: Week 2 = M5 Buy & Sell, Week 3 = M6 Send & Store, Week 4 = M7 Tax, Week 5 = M8 Sell Strategy. Delivered through the weekly coaching call.

B3 — 8-Step Strategy Process · Day 30–Day 90 (Weeks 5–12)

Starts AFTER B2 is complete (sequential). Pre-recorded course + same weekly coaching call. Culminates in 1:1 analyst session.

🚫 CEX Only Rule

No yield · No staking · No DeFi · No borrowing in L1

🔵

Level 2 — Advanced Track

Concurrent · Phase 2 + 3 in parallel from Week 2

A1 — Advanced Foundations · Week 1 of L2

One-off live session covering advanced Steps 1–4: macro correlations, mining economics, tokenomics, multi-entity structures

A2 — Advanced How-Tos · Weeks 2+ (one module/week)

Concurrent with Phase 3. Week 2 = M5 (4-Tier Yield Spectrum, staking as inflation offset), Week 3 = M6 (multi-sig), Week 4 = M7 (tax optimisation), Week 5 = M8 (exit ladders, borrowing). Via L2 weekly coaching call.

A3 — Advanced 8-Step Strategy · Weeks 2+ (concurrent)

Runs IN PARALLEL with A2 from Week 2. Pre-recorded + same L2 coaching call. 1:1 analyst session can be booked whenever ready — not gated to end of Phase 2.

Advanced Unlocks:

4-Tier Yield Staking DeFi Borrowing RWA Multi-Entity Tax Optimisation

The 8-Step Strategy Process (Phase 3)

The culminating exercise for both Level 1 and Level 2. Members complete a written strategy document — one step per session. "A strategy in your head is not a strategy. It's a vague intention."

STEP 01

Define Your Goals

Time horizon, capital targets, life goals tied to portfolio

STEP 02

Assess Your Risk Profile

Wealth Generation, Protection, or Income Creation. Capital sizing.

STEP 03

Build Your Investment Thesis

Why you hold each asset — written, first-principles conviction

STEP 04

Your Portfolio Allocation

BTC / altcoin / stablecoin split with rationale. Portfolio Allocation Calculator.

STEP 05

Create Your Buy Strategy

DCA amounts, frequency, automation setup, lump sum decision framework

STEP 06

Plan Your Tax Approach

Record keeping, software (Koinly/CTC), accountant briefing, CGT rules

STEP 07

Secure Your Assets

Security tier matched to portfolio size. Hardware wallet + seed phrase backup (L1). Multi-sig, estate planning (L2).

STEP 08

Write Your Sell Strategy

Written, pre-committed exit rules — targets, triggers, types. The #1 member pain point. Most powerful marketing hook.

L1 timing: Starts Day 30 (Week 5) after completing B2 how-tos. Works through steps via weekly coaching call. Culminates in 1:1 analyst sign-off. L2 timing: Starts Week 2 concurrently with A2 how-tos. Can book 1:1 analyst whenever ready.

Three Investor Types

Every member falls into one of three categories. The Shift OS tailors frameworks, allocation guidance, and worked examples to each.

💰

Wealth Generation

Growing capital significantly. Higher risk tolerance. Longer time horizon. Bitcoin core with altcoin satellites.

Examples: Ryan ($300K, wants to double), Harry (50% net worth in digital assets)

🛡️

Wealth Protection

Protecting existing wealth. Bitcoin-dominant (70–80%). Lower DCA amounts. Long-term thesis over price action.

Examples: Stacey (precious metals → Bitcoin), Nigel ($200K from property sale), Dina (senior)

📈

Income Creation

Generating yield on holdings. Stablecoin/DeFi strategies. Systematic withdrawal schedules. Available in Level 2 only.

Members asking about stablecoin yields, DeFi interest, term deposit alternatives
