Three Investor Types

Every member falls into one of three categories. The Shift OS tailors frameworks, allocation guidance, and worked examples to each.

💰

Wealth Generation

Growing capital significantly. Higher risk tolerance. Longer time horizon. Bitcoin core with altcoin satellites.

Examples: Ryan ($300K, wants to double), Harry (50% net worth in digital assets)

🛡️

Wealth Protection

Protecting existing wealth. Bitcoin-dominant (70–80%). Lower DCA amounts. Long-term thesis over price action.

Examples: Stacey (precious metals → Bitcoin), Nigel ($200K from property sale), Dina (senior)

📈

Income Creation

Generating yield on holdings. Stablecoin/DeFi strategies. Systematic withdrawal schedules. Available in Level 2 only.

Members asking about stablecoin yields, DeFi interest, term deposit alternatives
