Level 1

Beginner Track

Step 1 Foundations Crypto fundamentals & key concepts

Get oriented in the crypto landscape. Learn the fundamental concepts, terminology, and mental models you need before investing a single dollar.

  • Understand what blockchain and cryptocurrency actually are
  • Learn key terminology (wallets, keys, tokens, protocols)
  • Grasp the difference between centralised and decentralised systems
  • Build a mental model for evaluating digital assets

Step 2 Bitcoin Why Bitcoin matters & the investment case

Dive deep into Bitcoin — the original cryptocurrency. Understand why it exists, how it works technically, and the bull case for long-term investment.

  • Understand Bitcoin's value proposition and monetary policy
  • Learn how proof-of-work and mining secure the network
  • Evaluate Bitcoin as a store of value and investment asset
  • Recognise key on-chain metrics and market cycles

Step 3 Altcoins Digital assets beyond Bitcoin

Explore the broader digital asset ecosystem. Learn evaluation frameworks for altcoins, understand different token categories, and identify quality projects.

  • Understand the major altcoin categories (L1s, DeFi, infrastructure)
  • Apply a framework for evaluating altcoin fundamentals
  • Recognise red flags and common pitfalls
  • Build conviction through research, not hype

Step 4 How We Invest Portfolio construction & risk management

Learn the Collective Shift approach to building a crypto portfolio. Covers allocation strategy, risk management, and position sizing for beginners.

  • Build a diversified crypto portfolio from scratch
  • Understand allocation tiers (core, satellite, speculative)
  • Set risk parameters based on your personal situation
  • Learn dollar-cost averaging and systematic investing
● Live Open full module →
Step 5 Buy & Sell Exchanges & trading fundamentals

Master the practical mechanics of buying and selling crypto. Covers exchanges, order types, fees, and execution best practices.

  • Choose the right exchange for your needs
  • Understand order types (market, limit, stop-loss)
  • Minimise fees and slippage on trades
  • Execute buys and sells with confidence
● Live Open full module →
Step 6 Send & Store Wallets & secure storage

Learn to safely store and transfer your crypto assets. Covers wallet types, self-custody fundamentals, and security best practices.

  • Understand hot wallets, cold wallets, and hardware devices
  • Set up self-custody and back up your seed phrase
  • Send crypto between wallets safely
  • Protect yourself from phishing, scams, and loss
● Live Open full module →
Step 7 Tax Australian crypto tax obligations

Navigate Australia's crypto tax landscape. Understand your obligations, record-keeping requirements, and how to report crypto gains and income.

  • Understand CGT events and how crypto is taxed in Australia
  • Learn record-keeping best practices
  • Use crypto tax tools to generate reports
  • Know when to engage a crypto-savvy accountant
● Live Open full module →
Step 8 Sell Strategy Exit planning & taking profits

The most overlooked step — knowing when and how to sell. Build a personalised exit strategy before you need one.

  • Set clear profit-taking targets and rules
  • Understand market cycle timing and signals
  • Build a sell ladder and stick to it
  • Manage emotions during euphoria and fear

Level 2

Advanced Track

Step 1 Advanced Foundations Blockchain deep-dive & market structure

Go beyond the basics. Understand blockchain architecture, consensus mechanisms, and the macro forces shaping the crypto market.

  • Understand consensus mechanisms (PoW, PoS, DPoS, BFT)
  • Analyse market structure and participant incentives
  • Evaluate blockchain scalability trilemmas
  • Read whitepapers and assess technical design
● Live Open full module →
Step 2 Advanced Bitcoin On-chain analysis & advanced BTC thesis

Master on-chain analysis, mining economics, and the advanced macro thesis for Bitcoin as a global reserve asset.

  • Interpret key on-chain metrics (MVRV, SOPR, NUPL)
  • Understand mining economics and hash rate cycles
  • Evaluate Bitcoin in macro portfolios (institutional thesis)
  • Apply cycle indicators to timing decisions
● Live Open full module →
Step 3 Advanced Altcoins DeFi, L2s & tokenomics

Navigate DeFi protocols, Layer 2 scaling solutions, and advanced tokenomics. Learn to evaluate projects at a deeper technical and economic level.

  • Analyse tokenomics (supply schedules, vesting, dilution)
  • Understand DeFi primitives (AMMs, lending, derivatives)
  • Evaluate L2 solutions and their trade-offs
  • Assess governance models and protocol revenue
● Live Open full module →
Step 4 Advanced How We Invest Advanced portfolio strategies

Level up your portfolio strategy. Advanced position sizing, correlation analysis, and tactical allocation adjustments through market cycles.

  • Apply advanced position sizing and Kelly criterion concepts
  • Use correlation analysis to diversify effectively
  • Implement tactical vs strategic allocation shifts
  • Build portfolio models with risk-adjusted returns
● Live Open full module →
Step 5 Advanced Buy & Sell DEXs & execution strategies

Move beyond basic exchanges into DEXs, advanced order types, and execution strategies that minimise cost and maximise efficiency.

  • Trade on decentralised exchanges (Uniswap, dYdX, etc.)
  • Understand MEV, slippage, and front-running protection
  • Use advanced order types and limit strategies
  • Bridge assets across chains safely

Step 6 Advanced Send & Store Multi-sig & advanced security

Enterprise-grade security for your crypto. Multi-signature setups, advanced hardware wallet configurations, and inheritance planning.

  • Set up multi-signature wallets (Gnosis Safe, etc.)
  • Configure advanced hardware wallet features
  • Implement inheritance and succession plans
  • Audit your security setup for vulnerabilities
● Live Open full module →
Step 7 Advanced Tax DeFi tax, staking & complex scenarios

Navigate complex crypto tax scenarios including DeFi yields, staking rewards, airdrops, NFTs, and cross-chain activity.

  • Understand tax treatment of DeFi yields and LP positions
  • Handle staking, airdrops, and hard fork taxation
  • Manage NFT and cross-chain tax implications
  • Optimise tax position through strategic timing

Step 8 Advanced Sell Strategy Cycle analysis & advanced exits

Advanced exit frameworks using cycle analysis, on-chain signals, and systematic rebalancing strategies for maximising long-term returns.

  • Apply cycle analysis frameworks (Pi Cycle, Rainbow Chart)
  • Build systematic rebalancing triggers
  • Use on-chain signals for macro timing
  • Design multi-phase exit strategies for different holdings
