STEP 03 FOUNDATIONS SESSION (B1)

Altcoins

The mental model fix. Stop treating altcoins like stocks.

The most common mistake members make when they arrive — treating altcoins like equities. This module fixes that mental model completely and teaches how to evaluate digital asset projects using fundamental analysis. Includes the #1 gateway question (XRP) as a core pillar.

⚠️ HIGH IMPACT 4 TOPICS L1 3 TOPICS L2
🟢 LEVEL 1 CONTENT

Altcoins Decoded: Tech Startups, Not Stocks

The critical reframe. Delivered in 80%+ of member calls. The single most powerful mental model shift in the curriculum.

  • → Why altcoins have no equity ownership, no board governance, no regulatory vetting
  • → Why most altcoins fail — and why most don't recover to ATHs
  • → How altcoins differ from Bitcoin at the fundamental level
  • → The "good time, not a long time" framework for altcoin exposure

"Altcoins should really be called tech startups. The only similarity with Bitcoin is they both use blockchain technology. That's where it ends."

ANSWERS: "What altcoins should I buy?" · "Are altcoins like shares?" · "Why did my altcoin drop 80%?"

Fundamental Analysis Framework

How CS evaluates digital asset projects. The framework that separates real projects from hype.

  • → What to look for: team, product-market fit, revenue model, real users, community
  • → Red flags: no product, anonymous team, promises without deliverables
  • → The 20/80 rule: only ~20% of altcoins beat Bitcoin over full cycles
  • → HYPE (Hyperliquid) as case study: real product, real revenue, real users

ANSWERS: "How do you pick which altcoins to invest in?" · "What makes a good project?"

The XRP Reality Check

~60% of members ask about XRP. This pillar answers it definitively — with data, not opinion.

  • → XRP's business model flaws — why it doesn't work the way believers think

  • → Great marketing vs solid fundamentals — how to tell the difference
  • → Opportunity cost of holding XRP vs Bitcoin over time
  • → How to apply critical evaluation to ANY hyped project

ANSWERS: "Is XRP a good investment?" · "When will XRP go up?" · "Should I sell my XRP?"
