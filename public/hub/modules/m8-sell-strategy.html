STEP 08 PRACTICAL HOW-TOS (B2) ICP #1 PAIN POINT

Sell Strategy

The most emotionally challenging — and most important — module in the OS.

Directly addresses the ICP's #1 fear: no exit plan. Teaches members when and how to sell, reframes loss-cutting as a skill, and builds a systematic exit strategy. Level 2 adds advanced opportunity cost frameworks, cycle-aware exits, and borrowing as an alternative to selling.

🎯 SOLVES #1 FEAR 3 TOPICS L1 3 TOPICS L2 WORKBOOK INCLUDED
🟢 LEVEL 1 CONTENT

Loss-Cutting as a Skill

The Polygon story. Why the best investment decision isn't always a winning trade.

  • → Why selling at a loss is sometimes the best trade you'll ever make
  • → Opportunity cost: what is your capital doing while you hold a loser?
  • → Simon's Polygon story: cutting before 70-80% loss — the decision, the reasoning, the outcome
  • → The sunk cost fallacy applied to digital assets
  • → Emotional vs rational selling: how to separate the two

"My best investment decision wasn't Solana at 10x. It was getting out of Polygon before I lost 70-80%."

ANSWERS: "Should I sell my altcoin at a loss?" · "Will it come back?" · "I'm down 50% — should I hold?"

EVIDENCE: Greg went from $180K to $80K without cutting. Sunit asked "if this was your portfolio, what would you do?"

Re-Entry Framework

Selling isn't the end — it's freeing up capital for the next opportunity. But when and how do you re-enter?

  • → How to build a re-entry strategy after de-risking (written, not vague)
  • → What signals to watch for: risk-to-reward skew returning
  • → DCA back in vs lump sum re-entry — when each makes sense
  • → Patience as a strategy: waiting for value, not just price

ANSWERS: "I sold — when do I buy back?" · "How do I know when to re-enter?" · "What's the trigger?"

EVIDENCE: John/Johannes sold most holdings at $80K BTC break — asked for re-entry strategy. Said "patience" advice "wasn't sufficient."

Exit Strategy — Your Personal Sell Plan

A strategy in your head is not a strategy. This session builds your written sell plan.

  • → Building a written sell strategy: targets, triggers, rules (fill-in workbook)
  • → Partial profit-taking frameworks: how much to sell and when
  • → Bitcoin vs altcoin sell strategies — fundamentally different rules
  • → CS portfolio de-risk decisions as case studies: what triggered them

"A strategy in your head is not a strategy. It's a vague intention."

ANSWERS: "When should I sell?" · "How do I take profits?" · "How much should I sell?"

🔵 LEVEL 2 CONTENT

Unlocked after completing all 8 Level 1 modules.

Opportunity Cost Framework

  • → How to calculate opportunity cost of holding underperformers
  • → "What else could this capital be doing?" — the systematic portfolio review
  • → When to cut vs when to hold: objective, pre-defined criteria
  • → Systematic portfolio review process (frequency, what to look for)

ANSWERS: "Should I hold this loser or sell and redeploy?" · "What's the opportunity cost of holding?"

Cycle-Aware Exit Strategies

  • → How to build cycle-aware sell triggers (objective, written, pre-committed)
  • → CS portfolio de-risk decisions as case studies (what triggered them, when)
  • → Systematic profit-taking across multiple positions
  • → Rebalancing frequency and trigger conditions

EVIDENCE: John felt the "patience" advice wasn't sufficient — wanted clear, objective de-risk triggers

Borrowing as an Alternative to Selling

Advanced strategy: use your digital assets as collateral instead of selling. But only once you fully understand the risks.

  • → How collateralised lending works: using digital assets as collateral
  • → Loan-to-value ratios, interest rates, when borrowing makes sense
  • → Tax advantages of borrowing vs selling (avoid CGT events)
  • → Margin call and liquidation risk — the danger nobody talks about
  • → How to stress-test your loan against significant price drops
  • → Platform comparison: Ledn, Block Earner, and others

"If you don't understand liquidation, you shouldn't be borrowing."

ANSWERS: "Can I borrow against my Bitcoin?" · "What happens if Bitcoin drops while I have a loan?" · "Can I lose my Bitcoin?"

EVIDENCE: Growing interest in borrowing (Ledn, Block Earner) but zero understanding of liquidation risks

📋 Delivery Format

  • 🎯 L1 Weekly Coaching Call (1–2 sessions, ~45 mins each)
  • 📓 Workbook: Written sell strategy template (fill-in)
  • 📊 Case studies: CS portfolio sell examples and historical decisions
  • 🔵 L2: Risk assessment questionnaire before borrowing

  • ✓ (L2) Fully understand margin call and liquidation risk
