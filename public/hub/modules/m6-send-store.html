STEP 06 PRACTICAL HOW-TOS (B2)

Send & Store

Be your own bank. Secure your assets proportionally to what you're protecting.

The #1 unmet educational need identified across 30+ member calls. Teaches members how to secure their digital assets properly, with security advice that scales based on portfolio size. Level 2 adds multi-sig and institutional-grade practices.

🔑 #1 UNMET NEED 3 TOPICS L1 2 TOPICS L2 HANDS-ON SESSION
🟢 LEVEL 1 CONTENT

Self-Custody Fundamentals

The foundational shift: from trusting an exchange with your assets to being your own bank.

  • → What self-custody means and why it matters
  • → Hot wallets vs cold wallets: the key differences
  • → When to move off an exchange ("not your keys, not your coins")
  • → Exchange risk explained: why leaving assets on exchanges carries real risk

"The level of paranoia should be equal to the number of zeros you're trying to protect."

ANSWERS: "What is self-custody?" · "When should I move off the exchange?" · "Is the exchange safe enough?"

EVIDENCE: Nigel, Harry, and Dina all needed this in the first 30 days. Nigel: "I've no idea what you guys want me to set up."

Hardware Wallet Setup

Hands-on session. We'll do your first transfer together.

  • → Hardware wallet selection: Ledger vs Trezor vs Coldcard — which is right for you
  • → Unboxing and setup walkthrough (step-by-step)
  • → First transfer from exchange to hardware wallet
  • → Seed phrase backup: metal plate, secure location, testing recovery
  • → What happens if you lose your hardware wallet

ANSWERS: "Which hardware wallet?" · "How do I transfer?" · "What if I lose my seed phrase?"

EVIDENCE: Harry had SwiftX blocking BTC withdrawals to cold wallet. This is the #1 practical blocker.

Security Scaling

Your security level should match your portfolio size. A $500 portfolio and a $500,000 portfolio have different requirements.

  • → Security tiers by portfolio size: $1K → $10K → $100K → $1M+
  • → When to upgrade from exchange to hardware wallet
  • → Estate planning basics: who can access your digital assets if something happens to you?

ANSWERS: "Am I secure enough?" · "What about if something happens to me?"

EVIDENCE: Phil asked about estate planning. Dina has <$10K — completely different needs to Nigel with $200K.

🔵 LEVEL 2 CONTENT

Unlocked after completing all 8 Level 1 modules.

Advanced Cold Storage — Multi-Signature Security

  • → Multi-sig wallets: what they are and when you actually need one
  • → Setting up multi-sig for high-value portfolios
  • → Institutional-grade cold storage practices
  • → Passphrase security (25th word / hidden wallet)

Advanced Security & Estate Planning

  • → Geographical distribution of keys for maximum security
  • → Secure inheritance planning: how to ensure your digital assets are accessible after you're gone
  • → Using trusted third parties (custodians) vs full self-sovereignty
  • → Advanced seed phrase backup: metal plates, Shamir backups, vault services

ANSWERS: "How do I plan for my estate?" · "What security do I need at $500K+?"

📋 Delivery Format

  • 🎯 L1: Week 3 — ONE weekly coaching call (this module's week)
  • 📹 Self-paced: Video walkthrough + step-by-step PDF guide
  • 🤝 Hands-on: Members do first transfer during/after session

✅ Member Outcomes

  • ✓ Understand what self-custody is and why it matters
  • ✓ Hardware wallet set up with first transfer completed
  • ✓ Appropriate security level for their portfolio size
  • ✓ Seed phrase backed up securely
