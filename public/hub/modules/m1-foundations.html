STEP 01 FOUNDATIONS SESSION (B1)

Foundations

Why digital assets exist, how markets move, and what you're really investing in.

The entry point for every member. Sets the mental framework for everything that follows. Before you buy a single satoshi, you need to understand why digital assets exist and where we are in the cycle.

3 TOPICS LEVEL 1 LEVEL 2 2-HR LIVE SESSION
🟢 LEVEL 1 CONTENT

Why Digital Assets Exist

The core investment thesis — why this asset class exists and why it matters.

  • → Store of value, decentralisation, financial sovereignty
  • → The shift away from traditional finance — what's broken and why
  • → What makes Bitcoin different from every previous monetary technology
  • → The macro case for holding digital assets in a portfolio

"No one makes it in a single cycle. Stick it out."

ANSWERS: "Why should I be here?" · "Is this real or just hype?"

Market Cycles & Narrative Cycles

Understanding history so you have context for the present — and don't panic at normal market behaviour.

  • → Historical bull/bear transitions — 4-year cycle theory
  • → How major drawdowns played out (2014, 2018, 2022)
  • → What "normal" looks like in digital assets (80% drawdowns are normal)
  • → Why bear markets are the best time to accumulate
  • → Reading where we are in the current cycle
  • → Narrative Cycles: how capital rotates between sectors within a cycle (DeFi → NFTs → L2s → AI → RWA etc.)
  • → Signals vs Noise: what to actually track vs what to ignore (on-chain metrics, CS Filter indicators)

ANSWERS: "When will the market recover?" · "Is this a bear market?" · "Why is everyone talking about AI coins now?"

EVIDENCE: New members (Nigel, Stacey, Greg) have zero reference for bear/bull transitions or narrative rotation

Blockchain Ecosystems

Why there are many chains and how they relate to each other — so you stop thinking they're all "the same."

  • → Bitcoin chain vs Ethereum vs Solana as separate ecosystems
  • → Why different chains exist and what problem each solves
  • → Layer 1 vs Layer 2 basics (without the jargon)
  • → The "tech startups" mental model for understanding ecosystems

ANSWERS: "What's the difference between Bitcoin and Ethereum?" · "Why are there so many chains?" · "Which chain should I use?"

🔵 LEVEL 2 CONTENT

Unlocked after completing all 8 Level 1 modules.

Advanced Market Structure

  • → Macro correlations: interest rates, DXY, global liquidity cycles
  • → How institutional flows affect digital asset markets
  • → On-chain analytics: what the data tells us about market positioning
  • → Cross-cycle pattern recognition and long-term structural analysis

ANSWERS: "Why does Bitcoin move with stocks?" · "What are institutions doing?"

Advanced Ecosystem Analysis

  • → Layer 1 vs Layer 2 architecture in depth
  • → Ecosystem health metrics: developer activity, TVL, active users
  • → Cross-chain bridges and interoperability
  • → How to compare ecosystems using quantitative metrics

  • 📹 Self-paced: Recorded session + module summary PDF
  • 📊 Supported by: Research platform articles, 2026 Outlook post
