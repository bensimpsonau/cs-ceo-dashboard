## THE 12 MODULES

### 01 — Market Outlook & Current Context
Where we are right now. Key BTC levels, support/resistance zones. How quickly this market moves. Three macro scenarios and what each means for your portfolio. On-chain signals (NUPL, MVRV, STH cost basis, 200WMA). Institutional fund flows.
→ **Deliverable:** Written one-sentence market view

### 02 — Bitcoin & Crypto Landscape + Signal vs Noise
Why Bitcoin is fundamentally different to altcoins. BTC = digital gold, monetary network. Alts = venture bets. The AI/blockchain convergence. Narrative cycles (ICOs → NFTs → memecoins → AI). Why 98% of what CS does is say no. The patience principle.
→ **Deliverable:** Research time audit + noise sources identified

### 03 — Goal Setting
Define your "why" before allocating a dollar. Three goal types: wealth creation, preservation, income. How crypto fits your broader wealth picture. Time horizon. "A strategy in your head is not a strategy."
→ **Deliverable:** Written goal statement + target allocation + time horizon

### 04 — Bitcoin Deep-Dive
Why Bitcoin is the foundation. Decentralisation, fixed supply, network effect, institutional adoption. The long-term stack: DCA, 200WMA, 4-year cycle. When to buy: the "I will buy at X" framework. The feeling of fear. The confidence scale.
→ **Deliverable:** BTC allocation target + buy prices + DCA plan + conviction level

### 05 — Altcoin Deep-Dive
Narratives. Tokenomics and the supply trap. Good projects vs bad (HYPE vs Cardano). Use cases and daily active users. Catalysts. Marketing vs substance. 98% of things we don't invest in. Protecting downside. When to buy alts. Fear and conviction on alts.
→ **Deliverable:** Altcoin framework (which alts, filter criteria, max position size, exit criteria)

### 06 — How We Look at Investments
CS investment principles. Education not advice. Frameworks over predictions. Risk-first. Signals vs noise: on-chain data vs social media. What CS tracks and why.
→ **Deliverable:** Personal investment principles written

### 07 — Risk Management
Four risk types: market, security, liquidity, emotional. The 50% rule. Position sizing. Different risk profiles for different allocation sizes. Staged entry. The emotional cycle and cognitive biases (FOMO, loss aversion, confirmation bias, recency, anchoring).
→ **Deliverable:** Max drawdown tolerance + position sizing rules + bias audit

### 08 — Asset Analysis & Your Investment Thesis
The CS Portfolio: what we own, what we paid, and why. Deep-dives on each holding. The watchlist. What we're NOT investing in. Building YOUR investment thesis: targets, asset breakdown, entry/exit criteria, rebalancing rules, review cadence.
→ **Deliverable:** Written investment thesis (bring to analyst session)

### 09 — Yield & Staking
Two different things. Yield on stablecoins: 4-tier risk spectrum (Tier 1: 2-5% → Tier 4: 18%+). "Where does the yield come from?" Staking: offsetting supply inflation (ETH ~3-4%, SOL ~7%). Where to store cash/stablecoins.
→ **Deliverable:** Yield strategy decision (which tier, how much)

### 10 — Platforms, Wallets & Security
Where to buy by country (AU, NZ, CA, US). Exchanges for transacting, not storing. Hardware wallets. Seed phrase: 24 words, handwritten, two locations, never digital. Self-custody spectrum. The FTX lesson.
→ **Deliverable:** Security setup completed + tested

### 11 — Tax, Structure & Partners
🇦🇺 AU: CGT, 50% discount at 12+ months, SMSF, trusts
🇳🇿 NZ: Intent-based taxation, no formal CGT
🇺🇸 US: Long-term capital gains, Roth IRA, worldwide income
🇨🇦 CA: 50% inclusion rate, TFSA
Record keeping. Koinly / CryptoTaxCalculator. CS-vetted advisors.
→ **Deliverable:** Tax tool set up + advisor conversation booked

### 12 — Sell Strategy & Profit Taking
The most important module. The capstone. Sell strategy by goal type:
- **Cashflow:** Systematic selling for income
- **Wealth Creation:** Scale-out (10% at 2×, 15% at 3×, 20% at 5×). On-chain peak signals.
- **Wealth Preservation:** "If you've won, stop playing."
- **Altcoin-specific:** "Altcoins are a good time, not a long time." Tighter stops, faster profits.
"The best time to sell feels the worst."
→ **Deliverable:** Written sell strategy with specific % targets and price levels per position
